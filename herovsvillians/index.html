Superheroes

A superhero is a fictional character who possesses extraordinary powers or abilities and often uses them to protect the public or fight against villains and other threats. Typically, superheroes have a strong moral code and are driven by a desire to help others, often battling crime, injustice, and various forms of evil.

Supervillains

A supervillain is a fictional character who embodies evil or malevolence, often opposing superheroes and engaging in criminal or destructive activities. Supervillains are typically characterized by their desire for power, revenge, or chaos, and they often have elaborate schemes to achieve their goals.
