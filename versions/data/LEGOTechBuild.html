LEGO TechBuild

"TechBuild" is not an official LEGO theme or product line name, but it is used as an informal term for advanced LEGO building, particularly within the LEGO Technic community.

Informal Term: The term "TechBuild" appears in various fan and community contexts to describe building techniques and set references related to the Technic line.

LEGO Technic: This is the actual, current LEGO theme that offers a more challenging and realistic engineering experience using specialized elements like axles, gears, and pins to create functional models with working steering, engines, and other mechanisms.

Official Products: There are no current or discontinued official LEGO products named "TechBuild".

Building Techniques: The term also relates to discussions within the community about building methods, including "illegal" techniques that place stress on bricks, which are generally avoided in official sets to ensure durability.
