LEGO Minifigures Series 7

LEGO Minifigures Series 7 (item number 8831) was released in May 2012 and features 16 unique collectible characters in a distinctive scarlet red blind bag. The original retail price per blind bag was $2.99.

Characters Included

The series features a diverse set of characters from historical figures to modern-day people. Each minifigure was packaged with its own unique accessories, a display plate, and a collector's leaflet.

Aztec Warrior: Comes with a large, golden bird-like headdress, a spear, and a shield.

Bagpiper: Includes bagpipes, which were a new accessory for this series.

Bride: Features a wedding dress print and a bouquet of flowers.

Bunny Suit Guy: A popular mascot figure with a full rabbit costume and a carrot accessory.

Computer Programmer: A female character with a laptop and a mug.

Daredevil: Includes a helmet and a detailed outfit, one of the more popular figures.

Evil Knight: The first medieval villain in the CMF line, featuring black and silver armor, a sword, and a shield with a boar logo.

Galaxy Patrol: A futuristic space soldier with a blaster and removable armor, a counterpart to the classic Space theme.

Grandma Visitor: Depicts a character in a red cloak carrying a picnic basket, referencing Little Red Riding Hood.

Hippie: Features a colorful outfit and two flower accessories. Jungle Boy: Includes a monkey animal accessory, a dagger, and is part of the "Jungle" subtheme.

Ocean King: A mythical figure with a long beard and a trident accessory.

Rocker Girl: Features an electric guitar accessory.

Swimming Champion: A female swimmer with a swim cap and a medal accessory.

Tennis Ace: A female tennis player with a racket accessory.

Viking Woman: A strong female warrior with a helmet, sword, and shield.

Collectibility

As a retired series, these minifigures are no longer available in standard retail stores. Collectors often search for individual figures or complete sets on online marketplaces like eBay and BrickLink, where popular figures such as the Bunny Suit Guy, Aztec Warrior, and Roman Soldier are highly sought after.
