LEGO Tower

"LEGO Tower" refers to a popular mobile game, as well as several large-scale physical sets that feature towering structures, such as the Eiffel Tower and Avengers Tower.

The LEGO Tower Mobile Game

LEGO Tower is a free-to-play management and simulation mobile game developed by NimbleBit.

Gameplay: Players build and manage a skyscraper for LEGO minifigures to live and work in, building various apartments and businesses.

Features: The game includes collecting hundreds of unique minifigure pieces, unlocking special characters and vehicles, and visiting friends' towers.

Availability: It is available for download on both iOS and Android devices.

Notable Physical "Tower" Sets

LEGO has released several large, highly detailed physical sets that feature towers.

Eiffel Tower (10307):

Description: The tallest LEGO set to date, measuring over 58.5 inches (149 cm) high. It is a massive, detailed replica of the iconic Paris landmark.

Status: Currently available for purchase from the official LEGO Shop.

The Lord of the Rings: Barad-dûr™ (10333):

Description: A meticulously detailed replica of the Dark Tower from Middle-earth, measuring over 32.5 inches (83 cm) high, featuring a light-up Eye of Sauron and 10 minifigures.

Status: Currently available for purchase from the official LEGO Shop.

Avengers Tower (76269):

Description: A massive, 5,201-piece display model of the iconic Marvel building, featuring numerous characters and detailed rooms.

Status: This set is in the process of retiring and has sold out on the official LEGO US store, but may still be available from third-party retailers like Amazon.

Daily Bugle (76178):

Description: A tall, four-story building set featuring the Daily Bugle office block and 25 classic characters from the Spider-Man universe.

Status: This set has officially retired from the LEGO Shop but is still available from retailers like Amazon.

The City Tower (60473):

Description: A large, multi-story building set that serves as a central piece for a LEGO City layout.

Status: Currently available as part of the 2025 LEGO City line.
