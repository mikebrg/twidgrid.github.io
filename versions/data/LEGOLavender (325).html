LEGO Lavender (325)

LEGO Lavender (325) is a light, solid purple color (officially "Lavender" or "Lilac Solid" by the LEGO Group) introduced in 2012. It is an active color in the LEGO palette and is widely used across many themes.

Overview and Usage

Color Profile: Lavender (325) is a bright, solid lilac or light purple shade with the Element ID 325. It is one of several shades in the purple color family available to builders, sitting between the lighter end of the purple spectrum and the slightly darker Medium Lavender (324).

Availability: The color is available in a variety of standard LEGO parts, including basic bricks, plates, and slopes. It is used extensively across multiple themes.

Key Themes: The color is a popular choice for adding bright accents and is particularly prevalent in:

LEGO Friends: As with Medium Lavender, it is a common color in the Friends theme, contributing to the bright and varied aesthetic of Heartlake City.

LEGO Disney Princess: Frequently used in royal-themed sets, character outfits, and castle details.

LEGO Botanical Collection: Used for specific flower and plant elements to create realistic and diverse floral arrangements.

LEGO Classic: Included in many creative brick boxes, offering builders a wide range of colors for free-form building and imaginative play.

Examples of Sets with Lavender

Lavender is a versatile color used to add vibrant detail to many different models.

The Simpsons House (71006): Not a primary color, but some small internal pieces or specific details might use this color.

Mighty Dinosaurs (31058): The Creator line often includes a spectrum of colors, including Lavender pieces, in its varied sets for diverse build options.

Lucky Bamboo (part of the Botanical Collection): Specific elements in this set use the Lavender color for flowers or accents.
