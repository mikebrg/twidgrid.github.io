LEGO The Simpsons

The LEGO The Simpsons theme is a licensed product line based on the popular animated TV series, which was first introduced in 2014 to celebrate the show's 25th anniversary. After a period of retirement, the theme has recently been revived in 2025.

Construction Sets

The theme features detailed, large-scale models of iconic locations in Springfield, designed with numerous references to the show.

The Simpsons House (71006): This highly detailed set was released in February 2014 and recreates the family residence at 742 Evergreen Terrace.

Features: It has a removable roof and opening walls for easy access to the detailed interiors, including the living room, kitchen, and bedrooms. It also includes the family car.

Minifigures: Homer Simpson, Marge Simpson, Bart Simpson, Lisa Simpson, Maggie Simpson, and Ned Flanders.

The Kwik-E-Mart (71016): Released in May 2015, this set is a faithful brick-built parody of a typical American convenience store.

Features: Includes well-stocked shelves with fictional brands like Krusty-O's, a Squishee machine, refrigerated cases with frozen Jasper, and Chief Wiggum's police car.

Minifigures: Homer Simpson, Bart Simpson, Marge Simpson, Apu Nahasapeemapetilon, Chief Wiggum, and Snake (a.k.a. Jailbird).

Krusty Burger (10352): Released on June 4, 2025, as part of the LEGO Icons theme, this set marks the return of The Simpsons to LEGO.

Features: A detailed model of the iconic fast-food restaurant. It includes a side build of Homer's clown car from the episode "Coming to Homerica".

Minifigures: Homer Simpson, Bart Simpson, Lisa Simpson, a farmer-outfit Krusty the Clown, Sideshow Bob, the Squeaky Voiced Teen, and Officer Lou.

Collectible Minifigures

Two series of collectible minifigures from the show were released in "mystery" bags: Series 1 (71005) in May 2014, and Series 2 (71009) in May 2015.

Other Media

The Simpsons was also featured in the partially LEGO-animated TV episode "Brick Like Me" and in the LEGO Dimensions video game.
