LEGO Fortnite

The physical sets bring iconic Fortnite elements into brick form and include minifigures of popular characters. A special bonus of these sets is that each one includes a QR code to unlock a digital bonus within the LEGO Fortnite game.

Physical LEGO Fortnite sets

These four sets were released in the initial wave of the LEGO Fortnite collaboration.

LEGO Fortnite Battle Bus (77073): This set features the iconic Battle Bus and includes nine minifigures: Adventure Peely, Battalion Brawler, Brite Bomber, Cube Assassin, Cuddle Team Leader, Drift, Meowscles, Raven, and Trespasser Elite.

LEGO Fortnite Supply Llama (77071): Build the character-filled Supply Llama with this set, which includes multiple accessories.

LEGO Fortnite Durrr Burger Restaurant (77070): Recreate the famous restaurant mascot, complete with accessories and several minifigures.

LEGO Fortnite Peely Bone (77072): This set features a display-style model of the Peely Bone character.

Digital LEGO Fortnite game

LEGO Fortnite is a survival crafting adventure game available within the Fortnite platform. It features vast, open worlds and allows players to build structures, craft items, and survive either solo or with others.

Multiple experiences: The LEGO Fortnite space has multiple modes, including LEGO Fortnite Odyssey (the original survival mode) and LEGO Fortnite Brick Life (a social roleplay experience in a city setting).

LEGO Kits: In the game, players can purchase digital LEGO Kits from the in-game shop. These kits contain collections of builds, building parts, and decor items for use in their game worlds.

Playable on many devices: The game can be played for free on platforms that support Fortnite, including PC, PlayStation, Xbox, and Nintendo Switch.
