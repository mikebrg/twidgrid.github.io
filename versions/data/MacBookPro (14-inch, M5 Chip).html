MacBook Pro (14-inch, M5 Chip)

The 14-inch MacBook Pro with the M5 Chip was announced on October 28, 2025, and became available for purchase on November 4, 2025. This model introduces Apple's next-generation M5 architecture, featuring advanced AI capabilities and enhanced graphics performance, all within the existing unibody design.

Key Features and Connectivity

Apple M5 Chip: The M5 chip brings significant performance enhancements, focusing heavily on AI with a 32-core Neural Engine and improved power efficiency, providing faster speeds and enabling powerful new features in macOS and Apple Intelligence.

Design: The physical design remains consistent with the 2021/2023/2024 models, utilizing the aluminum unibody enclosure with the camera notch and ProMotion display.

Ports: The comprehensive port selection is retained for professional users:

MagSafe 3 power port

HDMI 2.1 port

SDXC card slot

Three Thunderbolt 4 / USB 4 ports (USB-C connector)

3.5 mm headphone jack.

Connectivity: Includes support for the latest Wi-Fi 7 (802.11be) and Bluetooth 5.4 wireless standards.

Camera and Audio: Retains the 1080p FaceTime HD camera, high-fidelity six-speaker sound system, and studio-quality three-mic array.

Battery Life: Due to the efficiency of the M5 chip, the 14-inch model offers enhanced battery performance, with Apple estimating up to 18 hours of video playback or 12 hours of wireless web use.

Keyboard: Features the reliable scissor-mechanism "Magic Keyboard" with physical function keys and integrated Touch ID.
