LEGO Minifigures Series 17

LEGO Minifigures Series 17 (71018) was a limited edition collection of 16 unique characters released in May 2017. This series is now retired and is no longer available for direct purchase from the official LEGO Shop.

Characters in Series 17

Each "mystery" bag contained one of the 16 characters, along with one or more accessories, a display plate, and a collector's leaflet. The series included a "mystery" character whose identity was initially concealed in marketing materials.

The 16 characters in the collection were:

Hot Dog Vendor

Elf Maiden

Butterfly Girl

Circus Strongman

Veterinarian

Battle Dwarf

Dance Instructor

Gourmet Chef

Connoisseur

Corn Cob Guy

Roman Gladiator

Rocket Boy

Pro Surfer

Retro Space Hero

Yuppie

The Highwayman (the mystery character)

Availability

Since the series is retired, availability is limited to secondary markets. You can find individual figures or complete sets through:

Online marketplaces: Sites like eBay and Amazon often have new and used figures available for purchase.

Specialized LEGO retailers: Various independent online stores that specialize in retired LEGO products and individual parts may also have these minifigures in stock.

Prices on the secondary market may differ from the original retail price depending on the character's rarity and condition.
