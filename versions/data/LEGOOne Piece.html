LEGO One Piece

Official LEGO One Piece sets

LEGO One Piece The Baratie Floating Restaurant (75640): The largest and most complex set, designed for adult builders ages 18+. The 3,402-piece set features the floating restaurant and includes 10 minifigures, such as Luffy, Sanji, and Dracule Mihawk.

LEGO One Piece The Going Merry Pirate Ship (75639): A 1,376-piece replica of the Straw Hats' first ship. The set includes multiple deck levels, interior spaces, and all five original Straw Hat crew members as minifigures.

LEGO One Piece Battle at Arlong Park (75638): Recreate the intense showdown between the Straw Hat and Arlong Pirates with this 926-piece set, featuring a collapsible pagoda and five minifigures.

LEGO One Piece Buggy the Clown's Circus Tent (75637): This 573-piece set includes a flippable tent and multiple circus contraptions. It comes with Buggy the Clown, Luffy, Zoro, and Nami minifigures.

LEGO One Piece Windmill Village Hut (75636): A 299-piece build that depicts the memorable scene of a young Luffy and Shanks. The set includes minifigures of Luffy, Shanks, and Makino.

LEGO One Piece BrickHeadz Monkey D. Luffy Figure (40660): A brick-built version of the Straw Hat crew's captain.

LEGO One Piece BrickHeadz Buggy the Clown Figure (40661): A brick-built figure of the pirate captain Buggy.
