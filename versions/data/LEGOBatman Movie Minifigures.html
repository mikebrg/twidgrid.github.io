LEGO Batman Movie Minifigures

The LEGO The Batman Movie theme featured two distinct series of collectible minifigures, each offering 20 unique characters from the film's extensive cast of heroes and villains. These limited edition series are now retired and can be found only through secondary markets. Series 1 (The LEGO Batman Movie 71017)

Released in 2017, the first series featured a wide range of quirky Batman suit variations and obscure villains from the DC universe.

Availability

These are limited edition, retired products and are no longer available for purchase directly from the official LEGO Shop. Collectors can typically find them on online marketplaces such as eBay and specialized LEGO part and minifigure retailers. Individual figures or sealed "mystery" bags/boxes can be found, with prices varying based on rarity and condition.
