LEGO Medium Lilac (268)

LEGO Medium Lilac (LEGO Color ID 268) is a current, official LEGO color introduced in 2003 for DUPLO and 2004 for the standard System bricks. It is often referred to as Dark Purple by the community on websites like BrickLink.

Color Details

LEGO Color Name: Medium Azur / Medium Azure

Common Name: Dark Purple (on BrickLink)

Color ID: 268

Hex Code: #441A91

RGB: 68, 26, 145 (approximate from Hex)

Status: Current (as of December 2025)

Notable Sets and Minifigures

Medium Lilac has appeared in a wide variety of themes since its introduction.

The Knight Bus: The color is prominently featured in all three versions of the LEGO Harry Potter Knight Bus sets (4866, 4695, and 4755).

DC Super Heroes: It is used in numerous early 2012 DC Universe Super Heroes sets, including The Batmobile and the Two-Face Chase (set 6864) and Superman vs. Power Armor Lex (set 6862), often in connection with villain vehicles.

Ninjago: The Skulkin and Serpentine vehicles from the early seasons of LEGO Ninjago utilized this color extensively.

Minifigures: The color is featured on several character minifigures and parts, including The Joker, Two-Face, Magneto, Stan Shunpike, and various alien/creature characters.

Availability

As a current and widely used color, Medium Lilac bricks can be purchased from various sources. Official LEGO Sources: Individual elements in Medium Lilac are available through the official LEGO "Pick a Brick" service.

Re-sellers: Online marketplaces such as BrickLink and BrickOwl have a vast inventory of individual parts in this color, available in both new and used condition.
