LEGO Wicked

The sets capture memorable characters and locations from the films, and some include QR codes to unlock online content and music. The first wave of sets mainly used "mini-dolls," while the second wave introduced standard minifigures for some sets.

October 2024 releases (based on Wicked)

LEGO Wicked Welcome to Emerald City (75684): This tower set offers a view of Emerald City and includes mini-dolls of Glinda, Elphaba, the Wizard, Madame Morrible, and Fiyero.

LEGO Wicked Elphaba & Glinda Figures (75682): A buildable display set featuring larger, posable brick-built dolls of Elphaba and Glinda, plus accessories.

LEGO Wicked Glinda & Elphaba's Dormitory (75683): A jewelry box-style set that opens up to reveal the dorm room at Shiz University. It includes mini-dolls of Glinda and Elphaba in their pajamas and daytime outfits.

LEGO Wicked Glinda, Elphaba & Nessarose at Shiz University (75681): A smaller play set that features the university building and Glinda's sailboat. It includes mini-dolls of Glinda, Elphaba, and Nessarose.

September 2025 releases (based on Wicked: For Good)

LEGO Wicked Emerald City Wall Art (75685): A 1,518-piece display piece for adult fans that depicts the Emerald City and includes minifigures of the main characters from both Wicked and The Wizard of Oz.

LEGO Wicked Elphaba's Retreat (75687): A smaller set recreating Elphaba's woodland hideaway and featuring mini-dolls of Elphaba and Fiyero.

LEGO Wicked Glinda's Wedding Day (75688): A set featuring the wedding scene with a special opening and closing butterfly mechanism. It includes a Glinda mini-doll with a changeable face.

LEGO Wicked Emerald City & Kiamo Ko Castle (75689): This set recreates the final scenes of Wicked: For Good and includes mini-dolls of Glinda and Elphaba, along with a flying monkey figure.

LEGO Wicked Glinda & Elphaba Visit Munchkinland (75690): Features Glinda's bubble and Boq's cottage, with mini-dolls of Elphaba, Glinda, and Boq.

LEGO Wicked Glinda & Elphaba Bookends (75691): A decorative set for bookshelves that includes standard minifigures of Glinda, Elphaba, Fiyero, the Wizard, Madame Morrible, and Pfannee.

LEGO BrickHeadz Wicked Elphaba & Glinda Figures (40794): A collectible set of BrickHeadz figures for Elphaba and Glinda.
