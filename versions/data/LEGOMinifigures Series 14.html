LEGO Minifigures Series 14

LEGO Minifigures Series 14 (item number 71010) was a special, limited-edition collection released in September 2015 with an exclusive monsters/Halloween theme. This marked the first time an entire collectible minifigure series was based on a single theme.

Characters Included

The series consists of 16 unique, spooky characters, each originally sold in a sealed "mystery" bag with its accessories, a black display plate, and a collector's leaflet. The full list of characters is:

Wolf Guy (Werewolf): Includes a bone accessory.

Zombie Pirate: Comes with a sword and a pirate hat.

Monster Scientist (Crazy Scientist): Features a beaker accessory.

Wacky Witch: Includes a broom and a black cat.

Plant Monster: Comes with two plant vines.

Fly Monster: Features claw hands and wings.

Specter (Ghost): Includes chains and a cloth cape.

Zombie Cheerleader: Comes with two pom-poms.

Tiger Woman: Features a whip accessory.

Gargoyle: Comes with unique headpiece and wings.

Skeleton Guy: Includes a trick-or-treat pumpkin bag accessory.

Monster Rocker: Comes with a red guitar.

Zombie Businessman (Zombie Businessman): Includes a briefcase and a newspaper.

Banshee: A ghostly figure.

Square Foot (Bigfoot): Comes with a camera accessory.

Spider Lady (Vampiress): Includes a small spider accessory and a plastic cape.

Collectibility

As a retired, limited-edition series from 2015, these minifigures are no longer available in standard retail stores at the original price. Collectors often search for individual figures or complete sets on secondary markets such as eBay and BrickLink, where prices are driven by demand and condition.
