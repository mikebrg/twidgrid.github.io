LEGO TT (Traveller's Tales) Games

Traveller's Tales (TT Games) is the primary developer behind the vast majority of LEGO video games since 2005, beginning with Lego Star Wars: The Video Game. Their games are known for a consistent formula of family-friendly cooperative gameplay, puzzle-solving, and a distinct, humorous tone.

History of TT Games LEGO Titles

TT Games' success began when they obtained the exclusive license to develop LEGO video games in 2004/2005 and shifted their focus from general platformers to licensed intellectual properties, which proved to be a massive success. Their final major release to date was Lego Star Wars: The Skywalker Saga in 2022.

List of LEGO Games Developed by Traveller's Tales (TT Games)

TT Games and its subsidiary studios (like TT Fusion) have developed numerous LEGO titles since 2005, based on popular franchises such as Star Wars, Indiana Jones, Batman, Harry Potter, Pirates of the Caribbean, Lord of the Rings, Marvel Super Heroes, Jurassic World, and The Incredibles. They also developed games based on Bionicle, Lego City, The Lego Movie, The Hobbit, Lego Dimensions, Lego Worlds, and Lego Ninjago.

Future Outlook

TT Games has transitioned to using the Unreal Engine for future projects after the release of Lego Star Wars: The Skywalker Saga. A new title, LEGO Batman: Legacy of the Dark Knight, is anticipated for release in 2026.
