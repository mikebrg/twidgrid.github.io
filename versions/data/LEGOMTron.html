LEGO M:Tron

The LEGO M:Tron (short for "Mobile Tron") theme was a popular, discontinued sub-theme of the LEGO Space line that ran from 1990 to 1992. It focused on a group of human space explorers and salvage workers who used modular vehicles to transport cargo and energy across the galaxy.

Theme Overview and Storyline

The storyline depicted the M:Tron faction as benevolent explorers and miners who discovered valuable "energy crystals" (transparent-red cones and plates). They were often in conflict with the villainous Blacktron II faction, who sought to steal their cargo.

Key features of the sets included:

Color Scheme: The vehicles featured a striking red, white, and transparent green color scheme, which has remained an iconic look for LEGO Space themes.

Magnets: A core play feature was the use of a magnet crane system. Almost every vehicle was equipped with a functional magnetic crane arm to pick up cargo containers and energy crystals.

Modular Cargo: The red cargo containers were designed to be easily attached and detached from vehicles using the magnets, allowing for efficient loading and unloading.

Minifigures: The minifigures wore white uniforms with red and black accents, featuring a stylized "M" logo.

Notable Discontinued Sets

These sets are now highly sought-after collector's items and are only available on the secondary market from re-sellers.

Mega Core Magnetizer (6989): The flagship set and main mobile base of operations, this large ship could separate into several smaller vessels and featured multiple magnetic cranes and a monorail-like transport system.

M:Tron Celestial Forager (6956): A ground-based rover and a small scout ship combined, featuring the magnetic crane system and large tires.

M:Tron Astro Explorer (6892): A ground vehicle with a large radar dish and the ability to transport a cargo container. Cargo Carrier (6862): A small, affordable set featuring a basic vehicle and a cargo container.

Legacy and Related Themes

Blacktron II (1991-1992): The primary criminal faction in this storyline.

Space Police II (1992-1993): The law enforcement faction that battled the Blacktron II faction.

Modern Homage: In 2023, as part of the LEGO Ideas program, a fan-designed M:Tron themed set was successfully crowdfunded and produced as a limited-edition product, demonstrating continued fan interest in the theme.
