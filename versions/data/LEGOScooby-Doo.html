LEGO Scooby-Doo

The LEGO Scooby-Doo theme was a licensed product line based on the classic cartoon franchise, available from 2015 to 2017. The theme featured a single wave of five main sets, each including characters from Mystery Inc. and a villain who could be "unmasked" to reveal their true identity.

Overview of the Theme

The LEGO Scooby-Doo line recreated the classic mystery-solving adventures in brick form, with sets focused on haunted locations and the iconic Mystery Machine van. All five core characters—Scooby-Doo, Shaggy Rogers, Fred Jones, Daphne Blake, and Velma Dinkley—were available across the different sets. The theme also included a promotional polybag set, video game integration with LEGO Dimensions, several animated web shorts, and direct-to-DVD movies like LEGO Scooby-Doo!: Haunted Hollywood.

Construction Sets

A total of six LEGO Scooby-Doo sets were released, including one promotional item. These sets are now retired and only available through secondary marketplaces.

Mummy Museum Mystery (75900): A smaller set featuring Scooby-Doo, Shaggy, and the Mummy/Dr. Najib. It included an opening golden sarcophagus.

Mystery Plane Adventures (75901): Focused on a plane build, with minifigures of Scooby-Doo, Shaggy, and the Headless Horseman/Elwood Crane.

The Mystery Machine (75902): This highly popular set featured the iconic psychedelic van with a removable roof and space for minifigures, plus Fred, Shaggy, the Zombie/Zeke, and Scooby-Doo minifigures.

Haunted Lighthouse (75903): A multi-level set including a lighthouse, speedboat, and an underwater rock cave, featuring Shaggy, Daphne, the Lighthouse Keeper/Verona Dempsey, the Swamp Monster/Mr. Brown, and Scooby-Doo.

Mystery Mansion (75904): The largest set in the theme, it included a clock tower, greenhouse with a man-eating plant, and seven minifigures: Shaggy, Velma, Daphne, the Vampire/Bob Oakley, the Ghost/Bluestone the Great, the Black Knight/Mr. Wickles, and Scooby-Doo.

Scooby-Doo (30601): A promotional polybag set released in 2016, featuring an exclusive Scooby-Doo minifigure.
