LEGO Aquaraiders

The term "LEGO Aquaraiders" can refer to a specific sub-theme of the discontinued LEGO Aquazone line from 1997, or the standalone "LEGO Aqua Raiders" theme from 2007. Both focused on underwater exploration and finding valuable items.

Aquazone: Aquaraiders I (1997, Discontinued)

This was a small sub-theme within the larger Aquazone world (1995-1998), which centered on different factions of undersea miners and thieves searching for crystals. The Aquaraiders were a single, neutral faction with only three sets, focused on their own mining operations.

Color Scheme: The sets and minifigures primarily featured a green and black color scheme.

Sets: Only three sets were released:

Crystal Scavenger (Set number: 2160)

Aqua Dozer (Set number: 2161)

Hydro Reef Wrecker (Set number: 2162)

Aqua Raiders (2007, Discontinued)

Released ten years later, this was a reinvented, standalone theme with no story connection to the original. It focused on a team of divers searching for lost treasure in the Bermuda Triangle and battling large, brick-built sea monsters.

Color Scheme: Vehicles mainly featured a yellow and black color scheme.

Sets: Seven sets were released in 2007:

Deep Sea Treasure Hunter (Set number: 7770)

Angler Ambush (Set number: 7771)

Lobster Strike (Set number: 7772)

Tiger Shark Attack (Set number: 7773)

Crab Crusher (Set number: 7774)

Aquabase Invasion (Set number: 7775)

The Shipwreck (Set number: 7776)
