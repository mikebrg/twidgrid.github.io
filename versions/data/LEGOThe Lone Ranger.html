LEGO The Lone Ranger

The LEGO The Lone Ranger theme was a short-lived, discontinued product line released in April 2013 as a tie-in to the Disney film of the same name. The theme was designed to bring back a Western-style building experience after a long absence and focused on a total of eight sets and promotional polybags.

Overview

The sets recreated scenes from the 2013 film, focusing on the adventures of the Lone Ranger and Tonto as they brought justice to the Old West and pursued the villainous Butch Cavendish and his gang. The theme featured many elements typical of classic Western sets, such as horses, wagons, trains, and buildings like a sheriff's office and a bank. Due to the commercial failure of the film, the LEGO theme was discontinued by the end of 2014.

Construction Sets

Six main sets were released, varying in size and complexity, plus two smaller promotional polybags.

Cavalry Builder Set (79106): A small set designed for building an army of soldiers, including a cannon, defense wall, and three cavalry soldiers, plus the Lone Ranger.

Comanche Camp (79107): Featured a teepee with a removable canopy, a canoe, and minifigures of the Lone Ranger, Tonto, and Red Knee.

Stagecoach Escape (79108): Included a detailed stagecoach with opening doors and an opening vault, along with five minifigures: the Lone Ranger, Tonto, Red Harrington, Jesus, and Barret.

Colby City Showdown (79109): Depicted a sheriff's office and bank with an opening vault and a rooftop cannon, featuring five minifigures: the Lone Ranger, Tonto, Dan Reid, Ray, and Frank.

Silver Mine Shootout (79110): A larger set with mine detailing, a secret waterfall entrance, a crane, and a dynamite catapult, including minifigures of the Lone Ranger, Tonto, Chief Big Bear, Butch Cavendish, and Kyle.

Constitution Train Chase (79111): The largest set in the theme, it included a locomotive, coal car, a wagon with a rotating gatling gun, a prison wagon, and a full set of train tracks. It featured seven minifigures, including the Lone Ranger, Tonto, and Butch Cavendish.

Lone Ranger's Pump Car (30260) and Tonto's Campfire (30261): Promotional polybag sets.

Collectibility

As a retired theme, the sets are no longer available in standard retail stores and are now sought after by collectors. Minifigures and complete sets can be found on online marketplaces.
