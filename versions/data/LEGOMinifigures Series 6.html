LEGO Minifigures Series 6

LEGO Minifigures Series 6 (item number 8827) was released in January 2012 and features 16 unique collectible characters in a sealed "mystery" bag format. The original retail price per blind bag was $2.99.

Characters Included

The series consists of the following 16 minifigures, each originally packaged with its own accessories, a display plate, and a collector's leaflet.

Classic Alien: Comes with a ray gun accessory.

Highland Battler: Features a sword and shield.

Sleepyhead: Includes a teddy bear accessory.

Lady Liberty: Comes with the iconic torch and book (tablet) accessories.

Bandit: Features two pistols and a bandana.

Flamenco Dancer: Includes a fan accessory.

Clockwork Robot: Features a wind-up key on its back.

Minotaur: Comes with an axe and a unique head mold.

Leprechaun: Includes a pot with three gold pieces.

Roman Soldier: Features a silver spear and shield, and is one of the more popular figures for army building.

Surgeon: Comes with a syringe and an X-ray tile.

Skater Girl: Includes a skateboard accessory.

Intergalactic Girl: Features a ray gun and a space-themed torso print.

Butcher: Comes with a cleaver and a T-bone steak accessory.

Mechanic: Includes a wrench and a toolbox.

Genie: Features a magic lamp accessory.

Collectibility

As a retired series from 2012, these minifigures are no longer available in standard retail stores. They are highly sought after by collectors, with popular figures like the Roman Soldier, Lady Liberty, and Minotaur fetching higher prices on online marketplaces such as eBay and BrickLink. The value of individual figures varies based on condition and demand.
