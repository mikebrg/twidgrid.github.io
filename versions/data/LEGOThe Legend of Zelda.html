LEGO The Legend of Zelda

The set was highly anticipated and allows fans to build a version of the Great Deku Tree from either Ocarina of Time or Breath of the Wild. It is aimed at adult builders and includes over 2,500 pieces.

LEGO The Legend of Zelda Great Deku Tree (77092)

This 2-in-1 display model was released in September 2024 and features two different building options.

Ocarina of Time version:

Build: The Deku Tree from the 1998 game, featuring a more sculpted head and a generous green canopy of leaves. Its mouth can be opened to reveal a dungeon inside.

Side builds: Includes Link's house from Kokiri Village.

Minifigures: Comes with Young Link and Adult Link, along with Navi the fairy.

Features: Posesable facial features and accessible side panels reveal items like the Ocarina of Time, Hylian Shield, and Master Sword.

Breath of the Wild version:

Build: A livelier, more colorful version of the tree, built on a platform.

Side builds: Includes a smaller build with Link and Zelda near the Deku Tree, as well as several Koroks and Hestu.

Minifigures: Includes Link in his champion's tunic and Princess Zelda.

Features: Includes posable facial features.
