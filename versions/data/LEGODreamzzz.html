LEGO Dreamzzz

The LEGO DREAMZzz theme, based on the animated series, features Mateo, Izzie, Cooper, and their friends defending the dream world from the Nightmare King. The sets are unique because their building instructions are story-led, inviting children to create fantastical creatures and vehicles by choosing between different building paths.

2025 sets

These sets were released in 2025 and are themed around the third season of the animated series, Enter The Cyber Game.

LEGO DREAMZzz Cooper's Gaming Controller Jet (71489): This 266-piece set is inspired by the TV series and lets builders create a video game jet or a transforming helicopter.

LEGO DREAMZzz Izzie and Bunchurro the Gaming Bunny (71490): Includes a buildable version of Bunchurro and a skateboard that can be rebuilt into a jetpack. The 252-piece set features game controller elements.

LEGO DREAMZzz Mateo vs. Cyber Brain Mech (71495): Help Mateo save a Dreamling from the Cyber Brain with this 368-piece set. You can build a colorful capsule that can be rebuilt into a Cyber Brain mech or a Cyber Brain scorpion mech.

LEGO DREAMZzz Cooper's Tiger Mech and Zero's Hot Rod Car (71497): This 1006-piece set includes a tiger mech and a hot rod car that can transform into a dragster.

LEGO DREAMZzz Mateo's Spray Paint Truck (71499): Build a 929-piece truck for Mateo with accessories like paint cans.

LEGO DREAMZzz Nightmare Shark Submarine (71500): Build a brick-built shark and then transform it into a hammerhead shark battleship or submarine.

Currently available sets

These sets were released in 2023 and 2024 and are still currently available, though many are noted as "retiring soon" on the official LEGO site.

LEGO DREAMZzz Mateo and Z-Blob the Knight Battle Mech (71488): A large 1333-piece set where Mateo and Z-Blob can be transformed into a knight battle mech.

LEGO DREAMZzz The Never Witch's Midnight Raven (71478): A 1203-piece set featuring the Never Witch's raven that can be rebuilt into a panther.

LEGO DREAMZzz Pegasus Flying Horse (71457): Build a flying horse with a carriage that can be rebuilt into a chariot, featuring a figure of Zoey.

LEGO DREAMZzz Crocodile Car (71458): Build a vehicle with crocodile-like details that can be rebuilt into a hovercraft or a swamp vehicle.

LEGO DREAMZzz Mrs. Castillo's Turtle Van (71456): Features a turtle-shaped van that can be rebuilt into a flying turtle or a submarine.

LEGO DREAMZzz Fantastical Tree House (71461): With 1,257 pieces, this set features a multi-level treehouse with multiple accessories and characters like Izzie, Cooper, and Mrs. Castillo.

Small and introductory sets

These smaller and lower-priced sets are a great way to start building the DREAMZzz theme.

LEGO DREAMZzz Z-Blob's Robot and Vehicle Adventures (71487): With just 121 pieces, this is a budget-friendly option for getting to know the characters Z-Blob and Mateo.

LEGO DREAMZzz Bunchu's Creative Animal Adventures (71488): A smaller set with 143 pieces that allows for different builds and includes the character Bunchu.

LEGO DREAMZzz Zoey's Cat Motorcycle (71479): Build a cool cat motorcycle that can also become a hover-backpack for Zoey. LEGO DREAMZzz Mateo's Off-Road Car (71471): A versatile off-road car that can take Mateo on road trips or adventures in the dream world.

LEGO DREAMZzz Mateo and Z-Blob the Robot (71454): This 237-piece set includes Mateo and Z-Blob, the robot, in different forms.
