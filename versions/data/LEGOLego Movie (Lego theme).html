LEGO Lego Movie (Lego theme)

The LEGO Movie theme is a retired licensed product line based on The LEGO Movie (2014) and its sequel The LEGO Movie 2: The Second Part (2019). The sets translated the films' core message of creativity and "Master Building" into physical toys, often featuring quirky, imaginative vehicles and locations.

Overview

The theme was notable for a few key aspects:

Diverse Settings: Sets represented the different realms visited in the films, including Bricksburg (city), the Wild West, Cloud Cuckoo Land, Space, and the post-apocalyptic Apocalypseburg.

Unique Builds: Many models featured a "build and rebuild" functionality, allowing the set to be transformed into different items, reflecting the Master Builder concept from the movie.

Iconic Characters: The sets prominently featured the main characters: Emmet Brickowski, Wyldstyle/Lucy, Batman, MetalBeard, Unikitty, and Benny the 1980s spaceman.

Status: The theme is now retired, and its sets are primarily available on secondary collector's markets.

Sets from The LEGO Movie (2014)

The original wave of sets launched in late 2013/early 2014 and expanded in 2015. Notable sets include:

MetalBeard's Sea Cow (70810): A massive, detailed pirate ship built from miscellaneous parts, one of the largest and most popular sets from the theme.

Benny's Spaceship, Spaceship, SPACESHIP! (70816): A classic space-themed set that captured the nostalgia of older LEGO themes and was named a "Dream Toy" in 2014.

Lord Business' Evil Lair (70809): The main villain's headquarters, complete with the Kragle and the "Think Tank".

Cloud Cuckoo Palace (70803): A bright, colorful set representing Unikitty's home world.

Double-Decker Couch (70818): A smaller, iconic set based on a recurring joke in the movie.

Sets from The LEGO Movie 2: The Second Part (2019)

The theme was revived in late 2018 and 2019 for the sequel, introducing new characters like Rex Dangervest and Queen Watevra Wa'Nabi.

Welcome to Apocalypseburg! (70840): The largest set of the sequel's theme, featuring over 3,000 pieces and a detailed recreation of the post-apocalyptic city built around the Statue of Liberty's head.

Rex's Rexplorer! (70835): Rex Dangervest's spacecraft that could split into smaller vehicles.

Ultrakatty & Warrior Lucy! (70827): A buildable figure of the battle-ready Unikitty from the new setting.

Emmet's Dream House/Rescue Rocket! (70831): A 2-in-1 build that transformed Emmet's house into a space rocket.
