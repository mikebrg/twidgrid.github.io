Microsoft Office Suite Office 2.0

Microsoft Office 2.0 was a 16-bit version of the office suite released in early 1992. It was developed for the Windows 3.1 operating system and was a minor update to the initial Office 1.0 and 1.5 releases.

Key Details of Office 2.0

Release Date: Early 1992.

Target OS: Windows 3.1x.

Architecture: It was a 16-bit application suite.

Successor: It was later superseded by Office 2.5 and then Office 3.0 (also known as Office 92) in the same year.

Applications Included

The Microsoft Office 2.0 bundle included the following core productivity applications:

Word 2.0a

Excel 3.0

PowerPoint 2.0

Microsoft Mail 2.1 (a license was included, but the software was not part of the physical bundle)

As with other early versions of Office, this software is now considered legacy and is no longer supported by Microsoft. Historical copies may be found in online archives for educational purposes.
