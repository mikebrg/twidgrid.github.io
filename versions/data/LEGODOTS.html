LEGO DOTS

The LEGO DOTS theme has been discontinued. It was an arts and crafts theme that used small, colorful 1x1 tiles to decorate wearable bracelets, bag tags, and room decor. After launching in March 2020, LEGO announced in January 2023 that the theme would be retired, with the last products exiting the portfolio by December 2023.

While no new DOTS products are being created, LEGO stated that elements of the play style, using decorated tiles, may appear in other themes.

Where to find LEGO DOTS

Since the theme has been retired, you can no longer purchase these sets directly from LEGO. Your best bet is to find them through secondary markets or other retailers that may have remaining stock.

Secondary marketplaces: Sites like eBay are a good option for finding both sealed and used DOTS sets. Specialty online stores: Some independent toy shops and brick resellers may still have a limited inventory.

Brick resale sites: On sites like BrickLink, you can find original parts and instructions for many of the individual DOTS sets.

Notable discontinued sets

The DOTS theme released dozens of sets over its short lifespan, ranging from wearable bracelets to customizable room decor.

Bracelets

These sets included a flexible bracelet and a variety of colorful tiles for decorating.

LEGO DOTS Unicorns Forever Bracelet (41802): One of the final bracelet sets, released in January 2023.

LEGO DOTS Rainbow Bracelet with Charms (41953): Included a red bracelet with colorful tiles.

LEGO DOTS Gamer Bracelet with Charms (41943): Featured gamer-themed tiles.

Home and room decor The line included numerous sets for decorating items like photo frames, pen holders, and storage boxes.

LEGO DOTS Creative Animal Drawer (41805): A buildable, rectangular storage drawer.

LEGO DOTS Cute Panda Tray (41959): Featured a panda-themed storage tray.

LEGO DOTS Hedwig Pencil Holder (41809): One of the final sets in the line, part of a collaboration with Harry Potter.

Bag tags and patches

These sets allowed kids to customize accessories they could take with them.

LEGO DOTS Bag Tags Mega Pack – Messaging (41949): Contained multiple bag tags and messaging-themed tiles.

LEGO DOTS Mickey Mouse & Minnie Mouse Back-to-School Project Box (41964): Featured Disney-themed bag tags and other items.

LEGO DOTS Adhesive Patch (41954): Included an 8x8 adhesive patch that could be stuck to surfaces.

Extra DOTS and multipacks

These were tile packs that could be used to expand existing sets or create new designs.

LEGO DOTS Extra DOTS Series 8 – Glitter and Shine (41803): One of the last extra tile packs, featuring glitter and shiny elements.

LEGO DOTS Ultimate Party Kit (41806): A large pack with many tiles and items like bracelets and cupcake decorations.
