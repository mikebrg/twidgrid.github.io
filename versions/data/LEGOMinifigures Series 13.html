LEGO Minifigures Series 13

LEGO Minifigures Series 13 (item number 71008) was released in January 2015 and features 16 unique collectible characters. The original retail price per blind bag was $3.99.

Characters Included

The series consists of the following 16 minifigures, each originally packaged in a sealed "mystery" bag with its own accessories, display plate, and collector's leaflet:

Classic King: Features a new hair and crown combo piece.

Sheriff: An Old West-themed figure.

Unicorn Girl: A popular and notable character in the series.

Snake Charmer: Includes a snake and a pungi instrument.

Goblin: A fantasy-themed character.

Paleontologist: A figure with a fossil or dinosaur bone accessory.

Alien Trooper: A sci-fi military figure, a counterpart to the Galaxy Trooper.

Egyptian Warrior: A historical character with a detailed headdress and spear.

Carpenter: Comes with a tool belt and a wooden plank accessory.

Evil Wizard: A dark sorcerer with a staff and potentially a crystal ball accessory.

Fencer: A sports-themed figure complete with a fencing mask and foil.

Samurai: A female variant of a classic warrior figure.

Disco Diva: Features a retro 70s/80s look with roller skates.

Hot Dog Man: A highly popular mascot figure with a hot dog costume piece.

Lady Cyclops: A mythological figure, a female version of a previous male Cyclops minifigure.

Galaxy Trooper: A sci-fi character, part of a space subtheme within the series.

Collectibility and Availability

As a retired series from 2015, individual minifigures or complete sets are no longer available from standard retail stores at the original price. They can often be found on online marketplaces like eBay or specialized LEGO marketplaces such as Brick Owl and BrickLink, where prices are determined by rarity and demand.

Notable figures like the Hot Dog Man, Classic King, Evil Wizard, and Unicorn Girl are particularly popular among collectors.
