LEGO Soccer/Football

The original LEGO Soccer/Football theme was a discontinued product line that ran from 1998 to 2006 as a sub-theme of the larger LEGO Sports line. While the interactive play-focused sets are retired, LEGO continues to produce modern, detailed display and playable sets related to the sport.

Discontinued Original Themes (1998-2006)

The original sets were designed to be interactive, tabletop games with minifigures on special stands that could be flicked to pass or shoot the ball.

Town/Football (1998): The initial sets were a brief part of the Town theme, often available as promotional items with sponsors like Shell. They included stadium accessories like stands, lighting towers, and medic stations.

LEGO Sports: Football/Soccer (2000-2006): This official theme was highly detailed, featuring branding from sponsors like Adidas and Coca-Cola, and minifigures of real-life players such as Zinedine Zidane.

Championship Challenge (3409): A large set featuring a full field, goals, and 12 minifigures for a two-player game.

Women's National Team (3416): A set from 2001 featuring six female minifigures.

Grand Championship Cup (3425): A large set that included 21 minifigures and could be expanded with field expansion sets.

Modern Football Sets (Current)

LEGO no longer produces a dedicated, continuous "Sports" theme, but it has released several standalone sets in recent years to celebrate football, often through the LEGO Ideas or Icons lines.

Icons of Play (40634): A current, playable set that celebrates women in sport and includes half a pitch, stands, a scoreboard, and 15 diverse minifigures.

LEGO Ideas Table Football (21337): A fully playable, buildable foosball table set featuring customizable teams with diverse skin tones and hairstyles.

Stadium Replicas: Highly detailed, large-scale display models of iconic stadiums:

Real Madrid – Santiago Bernabéu Stadium (10299)

FC Barcelona – Camp Nou (10284)

Manchester United – Old Trafford (10272)

BrickHeadz: The customizable FC Barcelona Go Brick Me (40542) and Manchester United Go Brick Me (40541) sets allow fans to create a personalized brick-built character in their team's jersey.

Minifigures: A limited edition series of collectible minifigures for the German national team (DFB) was released in 2016. Individual football player minifigures are also available from various third-party retailers.
