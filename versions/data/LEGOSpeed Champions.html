LEGO Speed Champions

LEGO Speed Champions is an auto racing-inspired theme of building sets, first launched in 2015, that focuses on creating authentic, brick-built replica versions of iconic classic and modern cars from well-known brands like Ferrari, McLaren, Porsche, and more.

Overview

Authenticity: The sets feature realistic design details inspired by their real-life counterparts, including sponsor stickers, specific aerodynamic features, and iconic liveries.

Scale: Initially launched at a 6-stud-wide scale, the sets switched to a larger, 8-stud-wide scale in 2020, allowing for more intricate detail and the ability to seat two minifigures in some models.

Target Audience: While designed for children (usually aged 9+), the high level of detail and authenticity also makes them popular display-worthy collectibles for adult car fans and builders.

Included Accessories: Most sets come with a driver minifigure in a racing outfit and sometimes include swappable accessories like different rim caps or tools.

Notable Sets (Current and Upcoming)

The 2025 lineup features a strong focus on Formula 1 cars, with one set available for each team from the 2024 F1 season, as well as classic supercars.

Ferrari SF-24 F1 Race Car (77242): A detailed replica of the 2024 F1 car, including sponsor logos and a rear wing.

Oracle Red Bull Racing RB20 F1 Race Car (77243): A model of the Red Bull Racing 2024 F1 car.

Lamborghini Revuelto & Huracán STO (77238): A double-pack featuring two distinct Lamborghini models for expanded play and display.

2 Fast 2 Furious Nissan Skyline GT-R (R34) (76917): A popular set based on the iconic car from the film franchise, including a minifigure of Brian O'Conner.

007 Aston Martin DB5 (76911): A set based on James Bond's classic car, featuring multiple swappable license plates.
