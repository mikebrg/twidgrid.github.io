LEGO Minifigures Series 11

LEGO Minifigures Series 11 (item number 71002) was released in September 2013 and included 16 unique collectible characters in green-accented blind bags. The original retail price per blind bag was $2.99.

Characters Included

The series features a diverse cast of characters inspired by history, sports, movies, and everyday life. Each minifigure originally came in a sealed "mystery" bag with its accessories, a display plate, and a collector's leaflet.

Scientist: Features two Erlenmeyer flasks and a female character design, which received positive attention in the media.

Lady Robot: A female counterpart to the Series 6 Clockwork Robot.

Evil Mech: A space-themed villain with armor and a blaster gun, featuring the classic Blacktron symbol.

Yeti: A mythical creature figure with a popsicle accessory.

Jazz Musician (Saxophone Player): Comes with a gold saxophone.

Holiday Elf: A Christmas-themed character with short legs, a present box, and a teddy bear accessory.

Constable: A classic British police officer figure with a baton and a traditional helmet.

Dirndl Girl (Pretzel Girl/Bavarian Lady): Comes with a pretzel accessory and a traditional German-style dress.

Diner Waitress: Features roller skates, a tray, and a strawberry ice cream soft serve.

Mountain Climber: Includes a curled up rope (new to this series) and a pickaxe.

Grandma: Comes with a grey cat and a brown basket.

Island Warrior (Tiki Warrior): Features a unique mask that covers his face and chest, and a spear.

Scarecrow: A farm-themed figure with a pitchfork and a black crow accessory (the crow piece was new to this series).

Barbarian: A fantasy warrior with two silver swords.

Welder: Comes with a new welding mask and welding tool attached to a cylinder.

Gingerbread Man: A food-themed mascot figure with a printed mug accessory.

Collectibility

As a retired series, these minifigures are no longer available in standard retail stores. The sets are now collector's items and can be found on secondary marketplaces like eBay and BrickLink. Certain figures, such as the Gingerbread Man, Scarecrow, and Yeti, are highly sought after by collectors due to their unique designs or relative rarity within a full box.
