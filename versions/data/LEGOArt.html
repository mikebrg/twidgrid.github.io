LEGO Art

The LEGO Art theme features mosaic-style sets for adults and older children, allowing builders to recreate famous artworks, pop culture icons, and other designs using 1x1 studs or tiles. Many sets include soundtracks to provide an immersive building experience.

Several new LEGO Art sets have been released in 2025.

LEGO Art LOVE (31214): Released in January 2025, this set is a brick-built sculpture of Robert Indiana's iconic pop art image. It is part of the theme's refreshed packaging design.

LEGO Art Vincent van Gogh – Sunflowers (31215): Released in March 2025, this 2,615-piece set recreates one of Van Gogh's famous paintings. It was developed in collaboration with the Van Gogh Museum in Amsterdam.

LEGO Art Keith Haring – Dancing Figures (31216): Released in May 2025, this 1,773-piece set lets you build five of Keith Haring's colorful dancing figures. It comes with five instruction booklets so you can build with friends and family.

LEGO Art The Fauna Collection – Tiger (31217): This 744-piece set, released in June 2025, features a tiger surrounded by flowers. It is part of the Fauna Collection and can be combined with other LEGO flower sets.

Popular and recent sets

These popular sets from recent years offer a great way to display your creative passion.

LEGO Ideas Vincent van Gogh – The Starry Night (21333): Released in 2022, this 2,316-piece set creates a 3D rendition of Van Gogh's masterpiece. It includes a Van Gogh minifigure and can be displayed freestanding or on a wall.

LEGO Art Mona Lisa (31213): This 1,503-piece set, released in October 2024, allows you to build a brick version of Leonardo da Vinci's famous portrait. It includes two sets of eyes so you can build her classic gaze or a more abstract version.

LEGO Art Hokusai – The Great Wave (31208): This 1,810-piece set from 2023 recreates Hokusai's iconic artwork with a unique 3D effect.

LEGO Art The Milky Way Galaxy (31212): Released in May 2024, this 3,091-piece set features a detailed, 3D brick-built recreation of the Milky Way. It is made up of five panels that can be built together.

LEGO DC Batman: The Animated Series Gotham City (76271): This large, 4,213-piece set from 2024 recreates the iconic Gotham City skyline and features detachable panels to reveal scenes from the animated series.

LEGO Art The Fauna Collection – Macaw Parrots (31211): Released in January 2024, this set includes two parrots and a tree branch.
