LEGO Minifigures Series 9

LEGO Minifigures Series 9 (item number 71000) was released in January 2013 in metallic silver blind bags and featured 16 unique collectible characters. The original retail price per blind bag was $2.99.

Characters Included

The series consists of the following 16 minifigures, each originally packaged with accessories, a display plate, and a collector's leaflet:

Waiter: Comes with a tray and a green wine bottle.

Cyclops: A mythical creature figure with a club.

Hollywood Starlet: Features a glamorous dress and a minifigure-shaped award trophy.

Heroic Knight: Includes a silver sword and shield.

Roman Emperor: A historical figure with a scroll printed with "VENI VIDI VICI".

Policeman: Features a detailed police cap, handcuffs, and a badge tile.

Chicken Suit Guy: A popular mascot figure with a full chicken costume, a highly sought after figure.

Roller Derby Girl: Comes with a helmet and roller skates.

Fortune Teller: Includes a hairpiece with a scarf and two tarot card tiles (sun and castle).

Judge: Features a white wig, a red robe, and a gavel. Alien Avenger: A sci-fi villain with armor and a blaster pistol.

Mermaid: A fantasy figure with a starfish accessory.

Battle Mech: A futuristic figure with detailed armor.

Mr. Good and Evil: A character with dual-printed face and torso representing both good and evil sides, with a purple potion bottle accessory.

Forest Maiden: A fantasy figure that can be used with Castle themes, featuring a bow and arrow and a shield with a tree emblem.

Plumber: A figure with a plunger accessory.

Collectibility

As a retired series from 2013, these minifigures are no longer available in standard retail stores. They are considered collectible items and can be found on online marketplaces, where prices vary. The Chicken Suit Guy is a particularly notable and popular figure among collectors.
