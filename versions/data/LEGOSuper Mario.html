LEGO Super Mario

The LEGO Super Mario theme features interactive, app-enabled playsets that let you create and play through your own Super Mario levels. An electronic Mario, Luigi, or Peach figure is required to play with the expansion sets, collecting digital coins and interacting with brick-built characters and obstacles.

2025 releases

2025 brings new sets to the LEGO Super Mario universe, including the new Mario Kart subtheme. The original interactive sets are reportedly planned to retire in 2026.

Mario Kart sets

These sets, released in 2025, are compatible with the interactive figures and let you create your own racing courses.

LEGO Super Mario: Mario Kart – Standard Kart (72032): This set features the classic Standard Kart on a display stand and is designed for adults.

LEGO Super Mario: Mario Kart – Mario & Standard Kart (72043): This set includes an interactive Mario figure along with the Standard Kart.

LEGO Super Mario: Mario Kart – Donkey Kong & DK Jumbo (72033): Includes a buildable DK Jumbo vehicle and Donkey Kong figure, along with a banana element.

LEGO Super Mario: Mario Kart – Baby Mario vs. Baby Luigi (72034): Set up a race between Baby Mario and Baby Luigi.

LEGO Super Mario: Mario Kart – Yoshi Bike (72031): Features a buildable Yoshi Bike with a Yoshi figure.

Other 2025 sets

LEGO Super Mario: Prince Florian & Castle Bowser (72042): Based on the game Super Mario Bros. Wonder, this set requires you to free Prince Florian from Castle Bowser. It also includes Pink Yoshi and other characters.

LEGO Super Mario: Wario & King Boo (72038): Features buildable Wario and King Boo figures.

LEGO Super Mario: Party at Toad's House (72041): Create a party scene at Toad's house with this set.

Retiring 2024 sets

The following sets were part of the 2024 lineup and are expected to be retired soon.

LEGO Super Mario: The Mighty Bowser (71411): This large-scale, 2,807-piece set for adults features a formidable, posable Bowser figure that can shoot a fireball.

LEGO Super Mario: Piranha Plant (71426): A collectible, display-focused set for adults featuring the iconic Piranha Plant character.

LEGO Super Mario: Super Mario 64 Question Mark Block (71395): This nostalgic set, also for adults, unfolds to reveal four classic Super Mario 64 levels inside.

LEGO Super Mario: Adventures with Mario Starter Course (71360): The original starter course, essential for interactive gameplay, is now retired.

Where to buy

Because many sets in this theme are retiring, availability will vary.

Official LEGO Stores: The best place to find the newest 2025 sets.

Online Retailers: Sites like Amazon still have many of the older sets in stock.

Secondary Marketplaces: Websites like eBay and BrickLink are your best bet for finding retired sets, though prices may be higher.
