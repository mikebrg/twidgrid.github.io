LEGO Medium Azure

LEGO Medium Azure (LEGO Color ID 322; BrickLink ID 156) is a vibrant, modern LEGO color first introduced in 2012. It is a bright, clear blue, often described as an opaque version of trans-light blue, reminiscent of a tropical ocean or clear sky.

Usage and Availability

Medium Azure was initially a signature color in the original LEGO Friends theme, where it was extensively used for water features and various building elements, such as the Dolphin Cruiser (set 41015).

It continues to be a current and widely available color in the LEGO palette, used across many different themes, including:

LEGO City (e.g., Arctic Explorer Ship (set 60368))

LEGO Creator (e.g., Toy Shop Town House (set 31105), Cozy House (set 31139))

LEGO Disney Princess (e.g., Elsa's Magical Ice Palace (set 41148))

LEGO Harry Potter (e.g., 4 Privet Drive (set 75968))

LEGO produces a wide range of basic and specialized parts in this color, from simple 2x4 bricks to specific arch and plate elements. You can purchase individual pieces in Medium Azure from official LEGO sources (like Bricks & Pieces) or from re-sellers such as BrickLink and BrickOwl.
