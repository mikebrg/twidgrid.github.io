LEGO Spring Yellowish Green (326)

LEGO Spring Yellowish Green (326), also known by the BrickLink name Yellowish Green, is a bright, solid green color with a distinct yellow tint. It was introduced in 2012 and is a current, widespread color in the LEGO palette.

Overview and Usage

Color Profile: Spring Yellowish Green is a vibrant, almost neon, yellowish-green shade that brings a natural, lively touch to LEGO builds. Its official LEGO Element ID is 326.

Availability: It is a common, active color used for a wide range of standard parts, including basic bricks, plates, tiles, and specialized elements like plant pieces and wings.

Key Themes: The color is widely used across many themes for adding bright accents or natural elements:

LEGO Classic: Frequently included in large creative boxes and supplemental sets for general building and color sorting activities.

LEGO Creator (3-in-1): Used as an accent color in sets that offer multiple build options.

LEGO Friends and LEGO Disney Princess: Often used for accent details in buildings or natural elements within the sets.

LEGO Ninjago: Featured in various sets for detailing, particularly for plant life and architectural accents.

LEGO Duplo: Introduced slowly into Duplo sets as well, making the color available to younger builders.

Examples of Sets with Spring Yellowish Green

Spring Yellowish Green is a versatile color that adds a distinctive pop to many different models.

The LEGO Movie Emmet's Dream House/Rescue Rocket! (70831): Features elements in this color as part of its imaginative build.

Mighty Dinosaurs (31058): The Creator line often uses a broad spectrum of colors, including Spring Yellowish Green pieces, in its varied sets.

Ninjago City Gardens (71741): Multiple elements in this detailed set, particularly plant pieces, use the Spring Yellowish Green color.

Animal Crossing and Trolls themes: This color has been used in newer themes for their unique aesthetic and characters.
