LEGO The Powerpuff Girls

The LEGO The Powerpuff Girls theme was a short-lived, discontinued product line launched in August 2018 and retired by the end of 2019. It featured only two standard building sets, along with expansion packs for the LEGO Dimensions video game.

Overview

The theme was based on the Cartoon Network animated series and featured minifigures of the main characters: Blossom, Bubbles, and Buttercup, as well as villains like Mojo Jojo and Princess Morbucks. The two physical sets focused on recreating action-packed scenes and locations from the show, like the playground and a jewelry store.

Released Sets

The full standalone theme consisted of only two sets:

Bubbles' Playground Showdown (41287): This set included a playground area with a slide, merry-go-round, and catapult, as well as a mech suit for Princess Morbucks. It came with Bubbles, Princess Morbucks, and Octi minifigures.

Mojo Jojo Strikes (41288): This set featured Mojo Jojo's getaway car, an ice cream stand, and a jewelry store. It included Blossom, Buttercup, and Mojo Jojo minifigures, along with a buildable Donny the Unicorn figure.

LEGO Dimensions Integration

Before the release of the standalone sets, The Powerpuff Girls characters were introduced into the LEGO Dimensions toys-to-life video game in September 2017.

Team Pack (71346): Included playable Blossom and Bubbles minifigures, plus buildable Octi and PPG Smartphone items.

Fun Pack (71343): Contained a playable Buttercup minifigure and a buildable Mega Blast Bot.

These packs unlocked an open-world area in the game and a battle arena based on Townsville.

Availability

As a retired theme, these sets are no longer available from the official LEGO Shop. They can be purchased through secondary market sites like eBay and other specialized brick retailers, where prices may vary depending on whether the set is new or used.
