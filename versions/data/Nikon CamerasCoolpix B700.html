Nikon Cameras Coolpix B700

The Nikon Coolpix B700 is a premium bridge camera designed for serious enthusiasts, featuring a powerful 60x optical zoom lens, 4K Ultra HD video recording, and the ability to shoot images in RAW format. Its DSLR-style body includes a vari-angle LCD monitor and a built-in electronic viewfinder, making it a versatile all-in-one solution for a wide range of photography styles.

Reviews and Performance

Image Quality: Images are noted for vibrant, saturated colors in bright, outdoor conditions. The ability to shoot in RAW format offers greater flexibility for post-processing compared to JPEG-only cameras. However, as noted by Photography Blog reviewers, image quality can be soft at 100% magnification and noise becomes significant above ISO 800 due to the small 1/2.3-inch sensor.

Video Quality: The addition of 4K video is a key upgrade, providing noticeably sharper and more detailed footage than 1080p. However, the 4K mode applies a significant crop factor, limiting the wide-angle view, and the zoom motor noise can be audible in recordings.

Handling and Features: Reviewers on Consumer Reports highlight the value of the electronic viewfinder (EVF) for composing shots in bright sunlight where LCDs often wash out. The DSLR-like ergonomics and deep rubber grip offer comfortable handling. The SnapBridge app is praised for seamless image transfer and remote control functionality via Bluetooth. The camera provides full manual controls (P, S, A, M modes), which is beneficial for photographers looking to grow their skills.

Zoom Performance: The 60x optical zoom provides an incredible focal range (24-1440mm equivalent), capable of capturing distant subjects that are barely visible to the naked eye. Image stabilization works effectively at moderate zoom ranges, but a tripod is generally recommended for sharp shots when fully zoomed in, as the AF system can "hunt" for focus at extreme telephoto lengths.
