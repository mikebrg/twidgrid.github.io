LEGO Minifigures Series 16

LEGO Minifigures Series 16 (set number 71013) was a collection of 16 unique, buildable minifigures released in September 2016. Like other collectible series, each figure came in a sealed "mystery" bag with one or more accessories, a display plate, and a collector's leaflet.

Character List

The series is well-regarded for its diverse and appealing character designs, including several popular costume figures. The full list of characters is as follows:

Desert Warrior: An Arabian Knight-themed figure with a printed shield and sword.

Penguin Boy: A minifigure in a full penguin suit, complete with a scarf and ice skates.

Ice Queen: Features an iridescent two-part cape, a unique ice crown, and two elemental ice blade accessories.

Spy: A figure in a black tactical suit with a night vision goggle accessory and a small laptop.

Banana Guy: One of the most sought-after figures in the series, featuring a full-body banana costume.

Hiker: A rugged outdoorsman with a backpack, map, and a walking stick.

Kickboxer: A female fighter figure with a helmet and boxing gloves.

Spooky Boy: A child dressed as a ghost, with a grey skin tone and a pumpkin trick-or-treat pail.

Rogue: A classic fantasy-themed figure with a hood and a detailed torso print.

Cyborg: A female sci-fi figure with one robotic arm and leg, and a detailed helmet.

Cute Little Devil: A boy in a red devil costume, complete with small wings and a pitchfork accessory.

Dog Show Winner: A glamorous character with a small, new dog figure and a large trophy.

Scallywag Pirate: A traditional pirate figure with a tricorne hat, a cutlass, and a map.

Babysitter: A civilian figure that included the second-ever LEGO baby minifigure.

Wildlife Photographer: A character with a camera accessory and a small, new animal figure (either a fox or a skunk depending on region/packaging).

Mariachi: A festive musician with a sombrero and an acoustic guitar accessory.

Availability

LEGO Minifigures Series 16 is a retired product and is no longer sold in "mystery bags" by official LEGO retailers. Collectors can acquire individual figures or complete sets through secondary market platforms.
