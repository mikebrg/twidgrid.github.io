LEGO Minifigures Series 15

LEGO Minifigures Series 15 (set number 71011) was a collection of 16 unique collectible minifigures released in January 2016. Each figure came in a sealed "mystery" bag with one or more accessories, a display plate, a collector's leaflet, and a unique in-game code for the LEGO Minifigures Online game.

Character List

The series featured a diverse range of characters, offering new parts and accessories for collectors and builders.

Queen: A regal figure that matched a classic King from a previous series, complete with royal attire and a scepter.

Animal Control Officer: Came with a skunk figure and a large net accessory.

Jewel Thief: A sleek character with a black jumpsuit, a diamond, and a rope launcher.

Tribal Woman: Notable for including the first-ever LEGO baby minifigure, which could be carried on her back with a special accessory.

Ballerina: Featured an elegant ballet outfit and a unique, solid plastic skirt piece.

Flying Warrior: A detailed warrior in gold armor with large, golden eagle-like wings.

Faun: A mythological creature with unique faun legs and a flute accessory.

Frightening Knight: A villainous-looking knight in dark green armor with a spiky mace and shield.

Wrestling Champion: A colorful wrestler with a championship belt and a trophy.

Shark Suit Guy: One of the most popular costume characters in the series, featuring an entire shark suit with an alternate fearful face print.

Clumsy Guy: A humorous figure in a banana peel shirt with crutches and a leg cast with "get well" messages printed on it.

Kendo Fighter: A Japanese martial artist with kendo armor (men and do) and two bamboo swords.

Astronaut: A modern take on the classic LEGO Space theme, featuring detailed torso/leg printing and a printed Classic Space flag.

Laser Mech: A futuristic character in a black and blue mech suit with trans-blue wings and a laser weapon.

Janitor: A realistic civilian character with a mop accessory.

Farmer: An updated farmer minifigure that included a spotted pig animal figure and a pitchfork.

Availability

As a retired product line from 2016, LEGO Minifigures Series 15 is no longer widely available from official LEGO retailers. Individual figures and complete sets can still be purchased from secondary market sources and online marketplaces.
