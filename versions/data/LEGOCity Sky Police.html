LEGO City Sky Police

The LEGO City Sky Police theme was released in 2019 and featured a number of sets focused on high-flying chases and air-based police operations. These sets have since been retired, but many are still available on secondary markets like eBay. Newer police sets under the main LEGO City theme also often include air vehicles.

Retired Sky Police sets (2019)

These sets were officially retired in 2019 but are often available through resellers.

LEGO City Sky Police Air Base (60210): The flagship set of the theme, this features a 3-level police station with a control tower, jail cell, and a storage area for vehicles. It includes a Sky Police plane, a jetpack with foldable wings, and several minifigures.

LEGO City Sky Police Drone Chase (60207): This smaller set includes a police helicopter with a firing net shooter to capture a crook's drone.

LEGO City Sky Police Parachute Arrest (60208): In this set, a high-speed police jet captures a crook who has bailed out with a working parachute. It also includes a police motorbike and a getaway buggy.

LEGO City Sky Police Diamond Heist (60209): This set features an armored police transport with lights and sound, a detachable jail cell, and a crook's helicopter.

LEGO City Sky Police Jet Patrol (60206): A smaller, 4+ set designed for younger builders, featuring a Sky Police jet with an opening minifigure cockpit.

Alternative LEGO City Police sets

For builders who prefer newer and still-available products, here are some alternatives that include police air vehicles:

LEGO City Police Station (60316): This 2022 set features a multi-story police station with a patrol car, a chopper, and a garbage truck that can bust through the prison wall.

LEGO City Police Helicopter Chase (60243): This set from 2020 includes a police helicopter with a magnet chain that can be used to lift the crooks' magnetic getaway vehicle.

LEGO City Police Helicopter (60275): This 4+ set is a simple, easy-to-build option featuring a toy police helicopter, a crook, and her water scooter.
