LEGO Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles

The LEGO Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles theme was a short-lived licensed product line that ran from late 2012 through the end of 2014, based on both the 2012 animated television series from Nickelodeon and the 2014 live-action film.

Overview and Key Features,

Characters: The theme included minifigures of the four main turtles—Leonardo, Donatello, Raphael, and Michelangelo—along with key allies and villains such as Master Splinter, April O'Neil, Shredder, and the Kraang. The turtle minifigures featured unique head molds designed specifically for the theme.

Sets: The sets included the Turtles' sewer lair, various vehicles like the Shellraiser and the Turtle Sub, and locations in New York City.

Discontinuation: The theme was eventually discontinued because Nickelodeon expanded its licensing partnership with another construction toy company, Mega Bloks.

Notable Sets

The theme included several memorable sets across its run:

Turtle Lair Attack (79103): One of the main sets from the 2012 animated series wave, it recreated the Turtles' underground hideout, complete with training areas and various accessories.

The Shellraiser Street Chase (79104): This set featured the Turtles' iconic heavily armored street vehicle and included minifigures for Michelangelo, Leonardo, Dogpound, a Kraangdroid, and a Foot Soldier.

Turtle Van Takedown (79115): Based on the 2014 live-action film, this set included the Turtles' custom van and was a popular choice for fans of the movie.

Big Rig Snow Getaway (79116): Also tied to the 2014 film, this set featured a large snow-themed truck and a chase sequence.

Collectibility

As a retired theme, these sets are no longer available in standard retail stores. The minifigures and complete sets are now sought after by collectors and can be found on online marketplaces, with prices varying based on the condition and rarity of the item.
