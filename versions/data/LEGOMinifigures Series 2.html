LEGO Minifigures Series 2

LEGO Minifigures Series 2 (item number 8684) was the second wave of the collectible minifigure product line, released in May 2010 in red blind bags. It featured 16 unique collectible characters. The original retail price per blind bag was $1.99 in the US.

Characters Included

The series included a diverse range of characters from history, fantasy, and everyday life. Each minifigure was originally packaged with its own accessories, a black display plate, and a collector's leaflet.

Spartan Warrior: A highly popular historical figure with an ornate helmet, spear, and large shield.

Witch: A fantasy character with a broom and a classic black pointed hat.

Vampire: A classic movie monster figure with a cape, a unique hairpiece with a widow's peak, and a small bat companion.

Surgeon: Comes with a surgical mask printed on the head and a scalpel accessory.

Traffic Cop: Features a detailed police uniform, a whistle on a string, and a red stop sign accessory.

Karate Master: A martial arts figure with a white gi and a black belt accessory.

Circus Ringmaster: Features a top hat, a whip, and a detailed ringmaster outfit.

Disco Dude: A 70s-themed character with an afro hairpiece and a silver record accessory.

Explorer: Features a safari hat and binoculars accessory.

Lifeguard: A female figure in a red swimsuit with a megaphone accessory.

Pharaoh: A detailed historical figure with an ornate headdress and a cobra staff.

Pop Star: A rock-and-roll-themed female figure with a microphone accessory.

Maraca Man: A musical character with maracas and a sombrero.

Weightlifter: Features a barbell accessory and a strongman outfit.

Mime: A classic mime figure with black and white makeup and a painted-on striped shirt.

Skier: A winter sports figure with two skis and poles.

Collectibility

As a retired series from 2010, these minifigures are no longer available in standard retail stores. The entire Series 2 line is highly collectible, with figures such as the Spartan Warrior, Pharaoh, and Vampire being particularly sought after on secondary marketplaces like eBay and BrickLink, often commanding premium prices due to their unique designs and popularity.
