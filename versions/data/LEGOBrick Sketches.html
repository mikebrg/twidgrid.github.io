LEGO Brick Sketches

The LEGO Brick Sketches theme has been discontinued. It featured brick-built portraits of iconic pop culture characters, designed to be hung on a wall or displayed on a desk. The theme was originally inspired by the work of LEGO designer Chris McVeigh. The last sets in the line were released in 2022, and the theme was phased out afterward due to low sales.

Because these sets are retired, they are no longer sold directly by LEGO. You will need to find them through secondary markets or retailers with remaining inventory.
