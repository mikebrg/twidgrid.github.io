LEGO Medium Lavender (324)

LEGO Medium Lavender (324) is a solid, medium lilac or purplish color that was introduced in 2012, primarily as a core color for the LEGO Friends theme. It is an active and relatively common color in the current LEGO palette.

Overview and Usage

Color Profile: Medium Lavender (officially "Medium Lavendel" in Danish) is a vibrant, solid purple shade. It is sometimes referred to by fans as dark pinkish purple.

Availability: It is available across a wide variety of standard LEGO parts, including basic bricks, plates, and specialized elements.

Key Themes: The color was introduced as part of the initial LEGO Friends launch and remains heavily used in that theme. It also appears in:

LEGO Disney Princess: Often used as an accent or a primary color in royal-themed sets and character outfits. LEGO Classic: Included in many creative brick boxes to offer builders a wide range of colors for free-form building and imaginative play.

LEGO Duplo: The color is also available in the larger Duplo bricks for younger children.

Examples of Sets with Medium Lavender Medium Lavender is a versatile color used to add vibrant detail to many different models:

Olivia's House (3315): This early LEGO Friends set was one of the first to feature the color prominently in its architecture.

Heartlake International School (41748): Part of the 2023 Friends relaunch, this set features elements in Medium Lavender, showcasing the color's continued use.

The Disney Castle (71040/43222): Large-scale collector sets based on Disney often include small details or character accessories in this color.

Mighty Dinosaurs (31058): While not a primary color, the Creator line often includes a spectrum of colors, including Medium Lavender pieces, in its varied sets.
