LEGO Aqua (323)

LEGO Aqua (323) is the official name for a solid color that is widely known in the fan community and on marketplaces as Light Aqua. This vibrant, pale green-blue shade was introduced in 2011 and is a currently active color in the LEGO palette.

Overview and Usage

Color Profile: Aqua (323) is a bright, solid, light cyan color. It is a versatile hue that adds a fresh and cheerful element to various LEGO models.

Availability: It is commonly found in many different types of LEGO elements, including basic bricks, plates, slopes, and specialized pieces.

Key Themes: This color is particularly popular in themes that focus on vibrant, modern, or natural aesthetics:

LEGO Friends: Used extensively in Heartlake City buildings and vehicles, contributing to the theme's bright, appealing color palette.

LEGO Disney Princess: Often appears as an accent or main color in underwater scenes or castle designs.

LEGO Classic: Frequently included in large creative boxes to provide builders with a broad spectrum of colors for general, free-form building.

LEGO Ninjago: Featured in certain detailed sets, such as the Ninjago City Gardens, for architectural details and water effects.

Examples of Sets with Aqua (Light Aqua)

Aqua is a versatile color used to add vibrant detail to many different models.

The Simpsons House (71006): Not a primary color, but some small internal pieces or specific details might use this color.

Mighty Dinosaurs (31058): The Creator line often uses a broad spectrum of colors, including Aqua, in its varied pieces for diverse build options.

Ninjago City Gardens (71741): Multiple elements in this set use the Aqua color for specific details.
