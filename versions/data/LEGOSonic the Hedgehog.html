LEGO Sonic the Hedgehog

There are many LEGO Sonic the Hedgehog sets available, featuring characters like Sonic, Tails, Knuckles, Amy, Shadow, and Dr. Eggman. The sets range from small, character-focused builds to larger playsets with interactive elements like a speed sphere launcher. 2025 and upcoming sets

These sets are available as of January and August 2025, according to Toys N Bricks and other LEGO news sites.

LEGO Sonic the Hedgehog Sonic's Campfire Clash (77001): Join Sonic by a campfire with his chili dog, but watch out for a G.U.N. Hunter and G.U.N. Beetle. This 177-piece set includes a speed sphere launcher for battling enemies.

LEGO Sonic the Hedgehog Cyclone vs. Metal Sonic (77002): Pilot Tails' Cyclone mech, which features posable legs that can be folded for flight mode. You can battle the included Metal Sonic minifigure in this 290-piece set.

LEGO Sonic the Hedgehog Super Shadow vs. Biolizard (77003): Recreate the final battle from Sonic Adventure 2 with the powerful Biolizard. The set includes Super Shadow and Super Sonic minifigures, a Biolizard figure with stud shooters, and a speed sphere launcher.

LEGO Sonic the Hedgehog Knuckles vs. Dr. Eggman Egg Crusher Mech (77005): Battle Dr. Eggman and his crushing mech with Knuckles. The 350-piece set includes Knuckles on a hoverboard and Dr. Eggman in his posable, egg-themed Egg Crusher mech.

LEGO Sonic the Hedgehog Team Sonic Command Truck (77006): Cruise through zones with this large, 747-piece truck set. The command truck features a removable cabin, a cargo area with a ramp for Tails' included ATV, and a launch function. It also includes Sonic, Tails, and Metal Sonic minifigures.

Currently available and retiring sets

The following are popular sets from 2023 and 2024 that are currently available, though some are noted as "retiring soon" on the official LEGO site. LEGO Ideas Sonic the Hedgehog – Green Hill Zone (21331): Released in 2022, this popular 1,125-piece set is designed for adult collectors and recreates the iconic Green Hill Zone. It features a classic-style Sonic minifigure, Dr. Eggman, Crabmeat, and a Motobug. LEGO Sonic the Hedgehog Super Sonic vs. Egg Drillster (76999): In this 590-piece set from 2024, Super Sonic and Shadow team up against Dr. Eggman's Egg Drillster. It comes with a speed sphere launcher and minifigures of Super Sonic, Shadow the Hedgehog, and Dr. Eggman.

LEGO Sonic the Hedgehog Knuckles and the Master Emerald Shrine (76998): This 325-piece set from 2024 features a shrine to the Master Emerald with buildable altar and minifigures of Knuckles and Amy Rose.

LEGO Sonic the Hedgehog Knuckles' Guardian Mech (76996): Released in 2024, this set includes a mech for Knuckles to pilot. The 276-piece build also features Rouge the Bat and the Master Emerald.

LEGO Sonic the Hedgehog Shadow's Escape (76995): Help Shadow the Hedgehog escape with his motorbike in this 196-piece set from 2023. It also includes a RhinoBot enemy.

BrickHeadz and keychains

LEGO BrickHeadz Sonic the Hedgehog (40627): A stylized, brick-built version of Sonic from the BrickHeadz line.

LEGO BrickHeadz Miles "Tails" Prower (40628): A companion BrickHeadz set featuring Tails.

LEGO BrickHeadz Knuckles & Shadow (40672): This BrickHeadz set, released in 2024, features a buildable version of both Knuckles and Shadow.

LEGO Sonic the Hedgehog Key Chain (854239): A miniature version of the Sonic minifigure attached to a keychain.

LEGO Shadow the Hedgehog Key Chain (854302): A miniature version of the Shadow the Hedgehog minifigure on a keychain.
