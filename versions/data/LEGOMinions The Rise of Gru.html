LEGO Minions: The Rise of Gru

Sets for the movie Minions: The Rise of Gru were released between 2020 and 2021. These have since been discontinued. The current LEGO Despicable Me theme features sets based on the film Despicable Me 4, which came out in 2024.

Retired sets are no longer available directly from LEGO but can often be found on secondary marketplaces like eBay and BrickLink.

Recently released Despicable Me 4 sets (2024)

These are the most current sets available, released in 2024 to celebrate the movie Despicable Me 4.

LEGO Despicable Me 4 Minions and Gru's Family Mansion (75583): An 868-piece toy house and tree house playset with 6 minifigures (Gru, Lucy, Margo, Edith, Agnes, and Baby) and 3 large, buildable Minion figures (Mel, Jerry, and Tim).

LEGO Despicable Me 4 Brick-Built Gru and Minions (75582): A large, buildable Gru figure surrounded by 5 dancing Minion characters on a rotating platform.

LEGO Despicable Me 4 Minions and Banana Car (75580): A 136-piece set that features a Minions' banana car, 4 Minions, and various accessories.

LEGO Despicable Me 4 Minions' Music Party Bus (75581): A 379-piece party bus that can be opened up to reveal a Minion party playset with a hot tub and DJ station.

Retired Minions: The Rise of Gru sets (2020–2021)

These are the sets that were released to coincide with the movie Minions: The Rise of Gru.

LEGO Minions: The Rise of Gru Brick-Built Minions and Their Lair (75551): This set lets you build brick-built versions of Kevin and Stuart. The model can then be opened to reveal a lair playset inside. Kevin can also be rebuilt into Bob.

LEGO Minions: The Rise of Gru Minions in Gru's Lab (75546): A 4+ set featuring Kevin and Otto in Gru's lab, with a slide, a ramp for a toy car, and a washing machine with a rotating function.

LEGO Minions: The Rise of Gru Minion Pilot in Training (75547): A plane-building set with Stuart (in a pilot outfit) and Bob minifigures, with a detachable roof for inside access.

LEGO Minions: The Rise of Gru Unstoppable Bike Chase (75549): Includes a motorbike and rocket skates, with minifigures of Gru, Bob, and Stuart.

LEGO Minions: The Rise of Gru Minions Kung Fu Battle (75550): A 310-piece set featuring a kung fu training temple with minifigures of Kevin, Stuart, and Otto.

LEGO Minions: The Rise of Gru Kung-fu Training (40511): A smaller promotional set that includes some Minions figures in kung fu outfits.
