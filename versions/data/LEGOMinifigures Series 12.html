LEGO Minifigures Series 12

LEGO Minifigures Series 12 (item number 71007) was released in October 2014 and featured 16 unique collectible characters. The original retail price per blind bag was $3.99.

Characters Included

The series consists of the following 16 minifigures, each originally packaged in a sealed "mystery" bag with its own accessories, a display plate, and a collector's leaflet:

Space Miner: Comes with an orange drill accessory.

Battle Goddess: Features an ornate shield and a pike.

Wizard: Includes a staff.

Dino Tracker: Comes with a bow and arrow and possibly a dinosaur egg accessory.

Swashbuckler: A pirate-themed character.

Jester: Includes an "Ace of Spades" and a "Joker" playing card.

Piggy Guy: A popular mascot figure with a piggy costume piece.

Hun Warrior: A historical warrior figure with a unique helmet.

Fairytale Princess: Includes a green frog accessory.

Pizza Delivery Man: Comes with a pizza box accessory.

Video Game Guy: Features a video game controller accessory.

Spooky Girl: A gothic-themed figure with a teddy bear accessory.

Prospector: Comes with a miner's pick.

Genie Girl: A magical character with a lamp accessory.

Rock Star: Includes a white electric guitar accessory.

Lifeguard: Features binoculars and a flotation device.

Collectibility

As a retired series from 2014, these minifigures are no longer available from official retail stores. They are considered collectible items and can often be found on online marketplaces like eBay or specialized LEGO marketplaces, where prices vary based on rarity and demand. The Piggy Guy and Spooky Girl are particularly popular and sought after by collectors.
