Government Services

Each of these platforms is tailored to its respective country's citizens and industries, helping streamline access to government information and services.

USA.gov

The U.S. government's official web portal provides access to federal, state, and local government resources, including benefits, jobs, and tax information.

U.S. Department of State

The official website of the U.S. Department of State, offering details about foreign affairs, travel advisories, and visa services.

Internal Revenue Service

The U.S. Internal Revenue Service platform where individuals and businesses can find tax forms, file returns, and access tax-related tools and resources.

GOV.UK

The United Kingdom's official government website providing information on public services, from health to tax to driving licenses.

U.S. Environmental Protection Agency

The Environmental Protection Agency's site in the U.S., focusing on environmental regulations, climate change information, and pollution prevention.

Australian Taxation Office

The Australian Taxation Office's website, supporting tax and superannuation information for individuals and businesses in Australia.

National Portal of India

The National Portal of India, offering a comprehensive gateway to Indian government services, citizen services, and e-governance initiatives.

French Administration

France's official site for public services, covering topics like administrative procedures, education, and health.

Government of Canada

The Canadian government’s central website for services such as immigration, public benefits, and business regulations.

China's General Administration of Customs

China's General Administration of Customs website, providing international trade policies and customs clearance guidance.

Government of South Africa

South Africa’s official government portal offering access to public services, business registration, and COVID-19 updates.

Government of the City of Buenos Aires

The website for the government of Buenos Aires, Argentina, focusing on local governance, public services, and community resources.
