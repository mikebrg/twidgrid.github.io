Entertainment & Media

Streaming services, gaming, movies, TV shows, music, comics, memes, and celebrity websites.

YouTube

The top global platform for video sharing, where users can upload, watch, and share a wide variety of video content including vlogs, music videos, tutorials, and more.

Netflix

A leading streaming service offering a vast library of movies, series, documentaries, and original programming accessible by subscription.

Fandom

A community-driven site where fans contribute detailed information on movies, TV shows, games, and pop culture. It’s a top resource for fan theories, character bios, and story summaries.

IMDb

A comprehensive source for movie, television, and celebrity information, including ratings, reviews, trailers, and more. IMDb is often used to research cast, crew, and plot details​.

Hulu

A popular streaming service known for its extensive library of current-season TV shows, exclusive series, and live TV options.

Disney+

The streaming service from Disney, offering movies, shows, and exclusive content from Disney, Pixar, Marvel, Star Wars, and National Geographic.

TikTok

A fast-growing social media platform for sharing short-form videos, popular for its entertainment, dance, comedy, and lifestyle content.

Spotify

Known primarily as a music streaming service, Spotify also hosts podcasts, artist radios, and exclusive content.

Rotten Tomatoes

A review aggregation site focused on movies and TV shows, featuring both critic and audience ratings to help viewers make informed choices.

Twitch

A live streaming platform for gamers and creators where users watch and interact with broadcasts on gaming, sports, music, and more.

Reddit

Although not solely entertainment-focused, Reddit hosts vibrant communities dedicated to virtually every form of entertainment, including movies, TV shows, gaming, and niche fandoms​.

The New York Times (Entertainment Section)

The NYT provides insightful analysis, reviews, and updates on the latest in entertainment, including movies, music, theater, and celebrity news​.
