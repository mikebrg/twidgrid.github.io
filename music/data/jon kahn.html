  • Jon Kahn is a Los Angeles-based singer-songwriter whose music has been featured in numerous films, television shows, and commercials. His recent single, “Fighter,” climbed to the top of the Billboard Digital Sales Chart and earned him recognition as Billboard’s #1 Emerging Artist.

    Kahn has collaborated with musicians from iconic bands such as the Foo Fighters, Creed, and The Wallflowers. He’s performed at renowned venues, including the Viper Room, Whisky a Go Go, The Roxy, and the Staples Center, where his band, The Color Green, opened for Creed.

    In recent years, Kahn has worked with some of Nashville’s top songwriters, including Hall of Fame writer Jeffrey Steele, Chris Wallin, Wynn Varble, Michael Farren, Phillip Lammonds, David Tolliver, Filmore, Kyle Schlienger, and Bobby Tomberlin. His forthcoming release, “Fingerprints,” is a soulful and introspective song that reflects his personal journey toward faith.
