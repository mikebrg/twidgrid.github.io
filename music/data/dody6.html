  • Dody6 (also known as Avenue GF) is a West Coast hip-hop artist from the Vernon neighborhood of Los Angeles. He gained significant national attention in late 2024 for his featured appearance on the track "hey now" from Kendrick Lamar’s album GNX

    He is a member of the Rollin' 40s Neighborhood Crips.

    Dody6 spent seven years in prison, during which he continued to make music, before being released around 2023–2024.

    His collaboration with Kendrick Lamar on "hey now" is his most high-profile work to date, where he trades verses with Lamar at the end of the track.

    Despite the success of the Kendrick feature, Dody6 revealed in April 2025 that he was experiencing homelessness and financial difficulties, though he expressed a mindset of "bouncing back".

    Dody6 is known for a prolific output of singles and albums, often released through the Too Lost record label.
