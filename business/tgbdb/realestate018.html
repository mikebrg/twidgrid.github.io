REAP - The Real Estate Agency Platform will enable your Real Estate Business to seamlessly manage your properties and contacts from an intuitive online Real Estate Software portal, allowing you to focus on what matters.

Accessible on any device, anywhere.

Realtors are always on the go, and so is REAP. Being accessible from any device with an internet connection, REAP allows Real Estate Agents to tap into their portal and access any data whenever, wherever.

Any feature available on REAP can be accessed securely through any web browser, without having the need to download any external applications.

Empowering Real Estate Agents

REAP was built keeping one thing in mind, the Realtor. By taking feedback from numerous Real Estate Agents from around the globe, REAP is a Realtor centric web solution for any size of Real Estate Businesses.
