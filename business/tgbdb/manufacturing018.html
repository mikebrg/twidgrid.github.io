JobBOSS²

JobBOSS², formerly known as E2 Shop promotes the profitability of scaling job shops, make-to-order manufacturers, and contract manufacturers by delivering a seamless quote-to-cash solution. A combination of JobBOSS expertise and the E2 Shop software, JobBOSS² eliminates waste, streamlines operations, and reduces bottlenecks while improving shop floor throughput. The cloud-based solution offers tools and integrations needed to run shop floor and back office operations. It helps manage the daily demands of job shops, get real-time visibility into job profitability, and gain accessibility, security, & efficiency with the cloud. It helps businesses become an ITAR/CMMC-compliant shop.
