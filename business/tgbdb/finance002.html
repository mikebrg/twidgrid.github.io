Xero

Xero is a cloud-based accounting software designed for small to medium-sized businesses. It offers easy-to-use features like invoicing, payroll, expense tracking, and financial reporting, all in real time. Xero’s intuitive interface allows businesses to stay on top of their finances and collaborate with accountants and teams from anywhere. It also integrates with over 800 third-party apps, streamlining business processes.
