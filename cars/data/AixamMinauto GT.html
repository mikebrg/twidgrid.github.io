Aixam Minauto GT

Production Timeline & Evolution

First Generation:

Produced: approximately 2011 to 2017.

Specs: Diesel engine ~400 cc (2-cylinder), about 4 kW (~5 hp), ≈14 Nm torque, top speed ~45 km/h, kerb weight ~425 kg.

Second Generation (Modern iteration):
Produced: from around 2018/2019 until early 2023.

Specs: Upgraded to a 479 cc 2-cylinder diesel delivering ~6 kW (8 hp) and 21 Nm torque; same top speed limit (45 km/h), weight ranging 425–500 kg depending on variant.
