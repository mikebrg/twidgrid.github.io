Aixam e-Crossover Premium

The Aixam e-Crossover Premium has been in production since 2023 and continues to be available as of 2025.

Aixam introduced the e-Crossover Premium in 2023 as a 100% electric, licence-free micro-SUV, catering to European drivers aged 14 and above, depending on local regulations. This model stands out as the first electric SUV in Aixam's lineup, offering a raised driving position and SUV-inspired design elements. It features a 6 kW electric motor and a 8 kWh battery, providing a range suitable for urban commuting. The e-Crossover Premium is equipped with modern amenities such as full LED lighting, a 3.5-inch TFT instrument panel, and safety features like automatic hazard lights activation during sudden braking .

In 2024, Aixam continued to refine the e-Crossover Premium, introducing enhancements in design and technology. The model remains available in 2025, reflecting its ongoing popularity and the brand's commitment to providing eco-friendly, accessible transportation options. With a starting price of €19,499, the e-Crossover Premium offers a blend of style, functionality, and sustainability for drivers seeking a compact electric vehicle without the need for a traditional driving licence .
