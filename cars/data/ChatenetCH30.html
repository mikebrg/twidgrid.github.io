  • Chatenet CH30

    2006-2017

    Chatenet CH30: The Specs

    Engine: Equipped with a 0.5L MPI engine, delivering approximately 20–21 horsepower.

    Transmission: Features a continuously variable transmission (CVT), ensuring smooth acceleration.

    Dimensions: Compactly sized at 3.065 meters in length, 1.567 meters in width, and 1.453 meters in height.

    Seating: Designed for two passengers, making it ideal for urban commuting.

    Safety: Underwent Euro NCAP testing, showcasing its commitment to safety in the microcar segment.

    Legacy & Discontinuation

    The CH30 was part of Chatenet's strategy to offer stylish, compact vehicles for markets with specific licensing regulations. However, due to evolving regulations and market demands, production ceased in 2017. Its design and engineering continue to influence Chatenet's subsequent models.

    Visual Appeal

    The CH30's design was a nod to retro aesthetics, with its rounded body and distinctive front grille. It stood out in a crowd, often mistaken for a miniature crossover.

    Parts & Maintenance

    For those looking to maintain or restore a CH30, parts are still available through various suppliers. From body panels to mechanical components, enthusiasts can find what they need to keep their microcar running smoothly.

    Final Thoughts

    The Chatenet CH30 remains a testament to French ingenuity in the microcar segment. While it may no longer roll off production lines, its legacy endures among collectors and enthusiasts.
