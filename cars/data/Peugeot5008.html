  • Peugeot 5008

    2009

    The Peugeot 5008 is a large, seven-seater crossover SUV known for its family-friendly practicality, bold SUV design, and upscale interior. The latest generation, launched in 2024, is available with a variety of electrified powertrains, including fully electric (e-5008), mild-hybrid, and plug-in hybrid options.

    Key Features

    The 5008 offers a versatile and high-quality cabin with a focus on space and technology.

    Seating and Modularity: It comes standard with seven seats in a flexible configuration. The third-row seats can be folded down or entirely removed, and the second-row seats are also collapsible, allowing for vast cargo space.

    PEUGEOT Panoramic i-Cockpit®: The interior features a striking, curved 21-inch HD display that appears to float above the dashboard, combining the instrument cluster and central infotainment screen.

    Practicality: Boot space is 348 litres with all seven seats in place, expanding to a generous 916 litres with the third row folded, and a maximum of over 2,000 litres with the second and third rows stowed.

    Driving Experience Reviewers note that while the Rifter prioritizes comfort and practicality over driving thrills, it handles well for a large family SUV. The fully electric version offers the quietest and smoothest power delivery, while the mild-hybrid's three-cylinder engine can be a bit noisy under heavy acceleration. The ride, however, can be a little firm and unsettled over rough surfaces.
