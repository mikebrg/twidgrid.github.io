Microcar M.Go 3 Dynamic

The Microcar M.Go 3 Dynamic was a specific trim level of the M.Go 3, a generation of microcars produced by Microcar (a subsidiary of Ligier). It was known for its stylish and well-equipped interior and exterior, aiming to provide a premium experience in the license-exempt market.

Key features

Production run: The M.Go 3 generation was introduced around 2015, and the Dynamic trim level was part of that era, produced until around 2016.

Engine: It was typically equipped with a 505 ccm Lombardini Progress diesel engine, which produced around 4 kW (5 horsepower) and had a maximum speed governed to 45 km/h (28 mph).

Exterior: The Dynamic trim often featured two-tone paint, alloy wheels, and chrome accents.

Interior: Inside, the M.Go 3 Dynamic offered a range of enhanced features for a microcar, including:

Radio with Bluetooth connectivity.

Central locking with a remote.

Electric windows.

Enhanced upholstery and finishing.

Size and capacity:

It featured a spacious interior and a large boot, with some configurations allowing for a loading length of up to 2.20 meters.

The vehicle measured approximately 301 cm long, 150 cm wide, and 156 cm high.

Safety: The M.Go series included safety features like reinforced door frames and front and rear disc brakes.
