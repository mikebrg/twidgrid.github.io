Ligier JS RC

The Ligier JS RC is a discontinued microcar that was manufactured by the French company Ligier and is known for its sporty and aggressive design. It was produced for several years, with models appearing around 2013 through 2017 and is now available on the used market. As a microcar, it can often be driven without a standard car driver's license in many European countries.

Key features

Design: The JS RC features a distinct, sporty body style, with some describing it as fun and attractive.

Powertrain: The vehicle was equipped with a small-displacement, 2-cylinder diesel engine with a continuously variable transmission (CVT), which is typical for Ligier microcars.

Engine specifications: According to vehicle information site Car.info, the 2014 Ligier JS RC was available with a 0.5 DCI or 0.4 DCI diesel engine, both producing 5 horsepower.

Equipment: While a basic model, it has been described in reviews as comfortable and relatively well-equipped for its class.

Target market: The JS RC was marketed toward younger drivers and city dwellers who want a compact, easy-to-park vehicle.
