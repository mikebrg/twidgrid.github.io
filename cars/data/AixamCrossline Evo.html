Aixam Crossline Evo

The Aixam Crossline Evo has been in production since 2016 and continues to be manufactured as of 2025.

The Crossline Evo is part of Aixam's "Emotion" range, which was introduced in 2016. This model is designed as a compact, licence-free vehicle, making it accessible to drivers from 16 years old in many European countries. The Crossline Evo is powered by a 479 cc Kubota engine, delivering approximately 8 horsepower. It features a modern design with a focus on comfort and safety, including options like ABS and automatic transmission. Over the years, the Crossline Evo has seen various updates, maintaining its popularity among those seeking a practical and stylish alternative to traditional vehicles.
