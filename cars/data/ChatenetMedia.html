Chatenet Media

The Media is one of the earlier models in Chatenet’s line of “without-licence” vehicles. It reflects the simpler, lighter designs before they moved to more modern styling and features in later models.

Because of its limitations (speed, engine output), it was mainly aimed at those needing minimal personal transport under regulations that permit driving with lighter licence or no full licence.

Technologically modest: automatic/CVT, simple features. Its lightweight construction and small engine helped meet regulatory requirements (both for licensing and safety/emissions) of its time.
