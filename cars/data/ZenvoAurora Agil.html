Zenvo Aurora Agil

The Aurora is powered by a 6.6-litre Mjølner quad-turbocharged V12 engine developed by Mahle Powertrain, making 1250 hp alone from the engine, plus 3 electric motors making 600 hp. The total power output is 1850 hp and 1700 NM of torque. The engine revs up to 9,800 rpm. It has a 7-speed hybridized automatic gearbox with paddle shifters.

The Agil is an extreme translation of Aurora, as the bodywork cleverly manages airflow. Delivering high levels of downforce, with a clear focus on track driving.
