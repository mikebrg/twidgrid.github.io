Aixam e-Coupé GTI

The Aixam e-Coupé GTI has been in production since 2024 and continues to be available as of 2025. This electric microcar is part of Aixam's eAixam range, which offers 100% electric, licence-free vehicles designed for urban driving.

The e-Coupé GTI is a stylish and compact electric vehicle that combines sporty aesthetics with practical features. It is equipped with a 3.5-inch TFT active matrix screen, a central multi-purpose on-board computer, and a digital clock. Safety features include scrolling front and rear indicators, full LED headlights and tail lights, and an automatic windscreen wiper. The exterior boasts a glossy black honeycomb grille, LED indicator lights on wing mirrors, and "GT"-style 16" black diamond-finish alloy wheel rims. Inside, it features carbon-look decorative inserts and a high-gloss black strip between rear lights.

As of 2025, the e-Coupé GTI remains a popular choice for those seeking a compact, eco-friendly vehicle that doesn't require a driving licence in certain European countries. Its combination of modern design, advanced features, and zero-emission driving makes it a standout in the microcar segment.
