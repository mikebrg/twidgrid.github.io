Chatenet CH28

The Chatenet CH28 is part of the French manufacturer's lineup of "voitures sans permis" (cars without a license). These vehicles are designed to be driven with a category AM license, which can be obtained at 14 years old in many European countries.

Engine Options

The CH28 is available with various engine configurations, catering to different preferences and regulations:

Diesel Engines: Some models are equipped with small diesel engines, offering fuel efficiency and longevity.

Electric Versions: In line with the growing trend towards sustainability, electric variants of the CH28 provide an eco-friendly driving experience.

Braking Systems

Depending on the model year, the CH28 has been equipped with different braking systems:

Coram System: Used up until the 2013/2014 model year, featuring 170mm or 210mm front brake discs and 100mm rear brake drums.

Gimec System: Introduced in the 2013/2014 model year, with 225mm front brake discs and 100mm rear brake drums.

Kailing System: The latest models feature this system with 225mm brake discs both front and rear, marking a first for any Chatenet model.

Dimensions

Length: Approximately 3.065 meters

Width: 1.567 meters

Height: 1.453 meters

These compact dimensions make the CH28 ideal for urban environments, offering maneuverability and ease of parking.
