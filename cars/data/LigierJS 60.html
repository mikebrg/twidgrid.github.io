Ligier JS 60

The Ligier JS 60 is a license-free, microcar-class SUV produced by the French manufacturer Ligier. With its compact design and low top speed, it is a popular option in Europe for drivers who do not hold a standard driver's license.

Key features

License-free driving: The JS 60, like other Ligier microcars, can be driven without a standard driver's license in many European countries. It is accessible to drivers as young as 14 or 16, depending on local regulations.

Engine options: The vehicle is typically offered with a choice of two Lombardini diesel engines:

Progress ACT: A 4-stroke, twin-cylinder 498cc engine with a maximum power output of 6 kW (about 8 hp).

DCI Revolution: A quieter, more fuel-efficient 4-stroke, twin-cylinder 478cc engine with the same 6 kW output.

Fuel efficiency: Both engine options offer excellent fuel economy, with consumption as low as 3.4 liters per 100 km.<,p>Technology: Depending on the trim, the JS 60 can be equipped with a large 10-inch touchscreen multimedia system that includes Apple CarPlay and Android Auto compatibility, as well as a reversing camera.

Safety features: For a microcar, the JS 60 includes several notable safety features, such as front and rear disc brakes for improved stopping power and a single-piece aluminum chassis with structural reinforcements.

Design: The JS 60 has an aggressive SUV-inspired design, with exterior features like a prominent grille, sculpted lights, and 15- or 16-inch alloy wheels. High-end trims feature a sportier look with carbon-style accents and sporty seats.

Trim levels: The vehicle is offered in various configurations, such as the entry-level "Chic" and the top-tier "Sport Ultimate," which offers more premium features.

Spacious interior: The two-seater cabin is noted for being spacious for its class and features a relatively large boot (storage compartment). Technical specifications

Maximum speed: 45 km/h (28 mph), in compliance with European microcar regulations.

Transmission: Automatic.

Length: 2,992 mm.

Width: 1,500 mm.

Height: 1,537 mm.

Operating Weight: 425 kg.

Uses and target market The Ligier JS 60 is designed for urban and suburban use. Its compact size makes it easy to park, and its fuel efficiency is ideal for short commutes. The license-free aspect makes it a practical solution for young drivers, the elderly, or anyone who needs a vehicle but does not possess a full driving license.
