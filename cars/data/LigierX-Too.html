Ligier X-Too

The Ligier X-Too is a two-seat, diesel-powered microcar (light quadricycle) manufactured by the French company Ligier from 2004 to 2011. The X-Too is part of Ligier's historical lineup and is only available on the used market. It is known for its compact size, maneuverability, and fuel efficiency.

Models and features

The X-Too was available in several versions, including the X-Too R and X-Too S, and also had more feature-rich "Max," "RS," and "Prestige" trims. Reviews from the time praised its handling and interior space. Some of the features included:

Engine: A Lombardini diesel engine, such as the 505cc FOCS Progress, which provided a smooth and quiet ride for a vehicle of its class.

Audio: The X-Too Max variant came with a multimedia system, which could include a radio and CD player.

Cargo: Reviewers noted that the trunk offered a generous amount of space for a microcar.

Specifications

The X-Too was developed to meet the regulations for license-free microcars in European markets. According to Ligier's Italian Wikipedia page, its factory specifications included:

Maximum Speed: 45 km/h (about 28 mph)

Engine Power: 4 kW (about 5 hp)

Weight: 350 kg (unladen)

Passengers: Registered for two people
