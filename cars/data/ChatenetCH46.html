  • Chatenet CH46

    2019

    The Chatenet CH46 made its debut in 2019, marking a fresh chapter for the French automaker known for its stylish and compact vehicles. This model was introduced as a successor to the CH26, offering a more modern design and updated features.

    Years of Manufacture

    Production of the CH46 began in 2019 and continues to the present day, with various updates and special editions released over the years. The model has seen updates in design and technology, keeping it competitive in the microcar market.

    Design & Features

    The CH46 is a two-door coupe with a sleek, modern design. Its compact dimensions make it ideal for urban environments, offering easy maneuverability and parking. The interior is designed for comfort and functionality, with features like air conditioning, a touchscreen infotainment system, and customizable seating options.

    Engine Options

    Under the hood, the CH46 offers various engine configurations, including:

    Diesel Engines: Typically 0.5L, providing efficient fuel consumption.

    Electric Options: For those seeking a more eco-friendly drive.

    These engines are paired with automatic transmissions, ensuring a smooth driving experience.

    Special Editions

    Chatenet has released several special editions of the CH46, each offering unique features and styling:

    CH46 Sportline R2: A sportier version with enhanced performance features.

    CH46 Touring: Focuses on comfort and luxury, with premium interior materials.

    CH46 RR: A high-performance variant with upgraded components.

    These editions cater to different preferences, allowing buyers to choose a model that best fits their lifestyle.

    Availability

    The CH46 is available in several European countries, including France, Italy, and Germany. Its compact size and efficient engines make it particularly popular in urban areas where parking and fuel efficiency are priorities.

    Conclusion

    The Chatenet CH46 stands out in the microcar segment with its stylish design, efficient engines, and versatile features. Whether you're navigating city streets or seeking a compact vehicle with personality, the CH46 offers a compelling option.
