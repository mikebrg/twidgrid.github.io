Peugeot Landtrek

The Peugeot Landtrek is a robust, body-on-frame pickup truck designed primarily for emerging markets in Africa, the Middle East, and Latin America, and is not available in Europe or North America. It is a versatile vehicle offered in single-cab and double-cab configurations, with a focus on functionality, durability, and off-road capability.

Key Features and Specifications

The Peugeot Landtrek is known for its spacious cargo bed, a practical interior with modern technology, and a range of engine options to suit various needs.

Cargo Capacity: It features one of the most spacious cargo beds in the market, with a width of 1.22 m between the wheel arches in the double cab version, a length of 1.63 m (2.43 m for single cab), and a depth of 500 mm.

Payload and Towing: The payload systematically exceeds 1 tonne, and can reach up to 1.3 tonnes depending on the version. It has a towing capacity of 3.5 tonnes with the new 2.2L diesel engine option.

Off-Road Capabilities: The Landtrek is available in both 4x2 and 4x4 configurations with an optional 4L mode and eLocker rear differential for enhanced traction. It has a maximum ground clearance of up to 235 mm and a wading depth of 600 mm.

Technology: The interior includes a 10-inch capacitive touchscreen infotainment system with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto compatibility, dual-zone automatic climate control, and a 360° camera system for easier maneuvering.

Engine Options

The Landtrek is available with both diesel and petrol engines, depending on the market.

1.9L Turbo Diesel: This engine produces 150 hp and 350 Nm of torque, and is paired with a 6-speed automatic or manual gearbox.

2.2L Multijet Turbo Diesel: A newer, more powerful option delivering 200 hp and 450 Nm of torque, available with an 8-speed automatic or 6-speed manual transmission.

2.4L Turbo Petrol: This engine offers 210 hp and 320 Nm of torque, available with manual or automatic transmission.

Market Availability and Pricing

The Peugeot Landtrek is currently sold in specific markets, including Latin America, Sub-Saharan Africa, the Middle East, Malaysia, Laos, and Ukraine. It is also rebadged and sold as the Fiat Titano in some regions and as the Ram 1200 in Mexico.

Pricing varies by market and trim level. In South Africa, prices start from around R388,900 for the single cab 4x2 model, going up to around R745,500 for the top-tier 4Action double cab 4x4 version, as of early 2025. In the UAE, the Landtrek 2.4T DC is priced at around AED 99,000.
