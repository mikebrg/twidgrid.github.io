Aixam e-Truck Drop Side

The Aixam e-Truck Drop Side has been in production since 2018 and continues to be available as of 2025. This electric utility vehicle is part of Aixam's e-Truck range, which includes both the Drop Side and Van models, catering to professionals seeking compact, eco-friendly transport solutions.

Introduced in 2018, the e-Truck Drop Side is designed for urban and peri-urban environments, offering a zero-emission alternative to traditional commercial vehicles. It features a 2m² loading surface with anti-slip wood curtains and double-wall rigid fold-down side walls, making it versatile for various business needs. The vehicle is powered by a lithium battery and boasts zero CO₂ emissions and minimal noise pollution, aligning with modern environmental standards.

Aixam continues to produce and sell the e-Truck Drop Side, reflecting its commitment to providing sustainable, licence-free vehicles for commercial use. The model's ongoing availability indicates its success and the growing demand for electric utility vehicles in the European market.
