Chatenet CH32

Design & Features

The CH32 stands out with its distinctive design, featuring a spacious cabin reminiscent of a mini sports car. It boasts modern amenities such as air conditioning, electric windows, alloy wheels, and a continuously variable transmission (CVT). The vehicle's front-wheel-drive layout and aluminum chassis contribute to its lightweight and agile handling.

Engine Options

Under the hood, the CH32 offered two engine choices:

Yanmar 2TNE68 Diesel Engine: A 523cc twin-cylinder engine producing 5.4 horsepower, known for its efficiency and reliability.

Electric Motor: For those seeking an eco-friendly option, the CH32 was also available in an electric variant.

Maintenance & Parts

Maintaining the CH32 is made easier with a range of available parts. For instance, the front pipe exhaust with a flexible joint is compatible with both the CH26 and CH32 models equipped with the Yanmar 2TNE68 engine.

Legacy

Although production of the CH32 ceased in 2017, it remains a notable example of French microcar engineering. Its blend of style, functionality, and compliance with European regulations for light quadricycles made it a popular choice among drivers seeking a compact and efficient vehicle.
