Chatenet CH26 v2

The CH26 V2 is a French microcar that falls under the category of "voiture sans permis" (car without a license), designed for drivers with a Class AM license in countries like France and Italy. It’s a two-seater coupe or convertible, measuring 3.065 meters in length, 1.567 meters in width, and 1.453 meters in height—small enough to make a Smart Fortwo look like a stretch limousine.
