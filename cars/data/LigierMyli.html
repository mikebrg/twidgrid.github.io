  • Ligier Myli

    2023

    The Ligier Myli is a microcar or quadricycle available with both fully electric and diesel powertrains, designed for urban environments. In many European countries, it can be driven by teenagers aged 14 or 16, depending on the local regulations for license-free vehicles.

    Models and pricing

    The Myli is offered in several trim levels, from the entry-level I.DEAL to the more sporty R.EBEL. The trim level determines the available features, customization options, and the size of the battery in the electric version.

    According to Ligier Latvia, the electric model typically starts at about €14,799, while the diesel version starts around €13,599. Specific prices vary by market, trim, and features.

    Powertrain and performance

    The Myli provides two engine options with different performance characteristics:

    Electric version: Features a 5.6 kW (7.5 hp) electric motor with a top speed capped at 45 km/h to comply with license-free vehicle regulations. It offers three battery options:

    4.14 kWh battery: Provides a range of up to 63 km.

    8.28 kWh battery: Provides a range of up to 123 km. 12.42 kWh battery: Offers an extended range of up to 192 km.

    Diesel version: Powered by a 499.8 cc, single-cylinder diesel engine with a power output of 6 kW (8 hp) and torque of 27 Nm. It has a fuel consumption of 3.0 L/100 km.

    Dimensions and features

    The Myli is noted for its compact size and features that make it well-suited for city driving.

    Dimensions Length: 2,958 mm; Width: 1,499 mm; Height: 1,541 mm

    Trunk space: 459 liters, which is a significant amount for its class

    Infotainment: Optional 10-inch touchscreen with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto. Base models may have a simpler 1DIN radio.

    Comfort: Available features include power steering, heated driver's seat, air conditioning, and a heater/defroster.

    Interior: Designed with a modern, clean dashboard and can be equipped with carbon-look or body-colored finishes.

    Safety Includes reverse parking camera and disc brakes on both the front and rear wheels.

    Wheels: Available in different sizes depending on the trim level, ranging from 13 to 16 inches.

    Charging (EV): Charges via a standard 230V household socket. A full charge from empty takes about 7 hours on some models.
