Microcar M.Go Outdoor

The Microcar M.Go Outdoor was a special edition of the M.Go series, known for its rugged and adventurous styling. Produced by Microcar (a subsidiary of Ligier), this version was designed to appear more robust and suited for outdoor activities, particularly in rural environments.

Distinctive features

"Outdoor" styling: The M.Go Outdoor featured an off-road inspired look with exclusive equipment, including front and rear "bullbars".

Engine: It was equipped with the standard microcar diesel engine of its time. Some models came with a Lombardini DCI REVOLUTION engine, offering higher performance than earlier versions.

Interior: The cabin was well-equipped, featuring a radio with MP3 functionality, and was designed with clever storage solutions.

Large storage capacity: A key feature of the M.Go series was its large boot, and the Outdoor trim was no exception. It offered a loading length of up to 2.20 meters and a massive 1,500-liter boot space with the passenger seat folded down.

Unique touches:

15" alloy wheels with a grey metallic finish.

LED rear lights.

Rear parking sensors.

Available in special colors, such as Bronze Metal for the M.Go Highland X.

Legacy

The M.Go Outdoor was one of several trim levels designed to diversify the M.Go lineup and appeal to different customer tastes. The "Outdoor" design approach was later adopted by other models in the Ligier-Microcar range, such as the M.Go Highland.
