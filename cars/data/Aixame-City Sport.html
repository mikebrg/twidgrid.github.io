  • Aixam e-City Sport

    2021

    The Aixam e-City Sport has been in production since 2021 and continues to be available as of 2025. This compact, fully electric microcar is part of Aixam's e-City range, which has been evolving since the introduction of the Mega e-City in 2007. The e-City Sport model offers a sporty design with features like a glossy black front grille, dual tailpipes, and 15' black alloy wheels. It's designed to be agile and easy to handle, making it ideal for urban environments.

    Aixam, a French automobile manufacturer, has been producing microcars since 1983. The company has continually innovated in the quadricycle market, introducing various models over the years. The e-City range, including the Sport variant, represents Aixam's commitment to electric mobility, offering vehicles that are both environmentally friendly and accessible to a wide range of drivers. These cars are particularly popular in European countries where driving licences for quadricycles can be obtained at a younger age, such as 15, making them an attractive option for new drivers.

    In summary, the Aixam e-City Sport is a modern, electric microcar that combines style, practicality, and sustainability. Its continued production reflects the growing demand for compact, eco-friendly vehicles in urban settings. Whether you're a young driver looking for your first car or someone seeking an efficient city vehicle, the e-City Sport offers a compelling choice.
