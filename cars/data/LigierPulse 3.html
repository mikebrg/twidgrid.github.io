Ligier Pulse 3

The Ligier Pulse 3 is an electric three-wheeled scooter designed for professional use, particularly last-mile delivery and postal services. It is equipped with a patented tilting system that provides stability for the driver while keeping the cargo bay level.

Models and specifications

The Ligier Pulse 3 is available in different versions, which primarily differ in power output and maximum speed.

Feature: L2e Version; L5e Version

Battery capacity: Up to 4.8 kW; Up to 7.5 kW or 8.28 kW

Top speed: 45 km/h; 90 km/h

Range: Up to 110 km; Up to 109 km

Driver's license: Moped (AM) license; Motorcycle (up to 125 cc) or car (B) license

Payload capacity Up to 65 kg, with storage space both front and rear. Note that some sources cite a lower maximum payload.

Total payload up to 80 kg (580 liters). Note that some sources cite a lower maximum payload.

Dimensions (approximate): Length: 2,258 mm; Length: 2,258 mm

Key features

Patented pendulum system: The driver's controls and seat can tilt up to 30 degrees while the rear storage compartment remains stable. This improves maneuverability and safety.

Reinforced chassis: The Pulse 3 is built with a reinforced chassis for durability, especially under intensive use.

Customizable: The vehicle can be customized to suit specific needs.

Ergonomic design: The driving position was developed with input from occupational ergonomists.

Overnight charging: The battery can be charged overnight using a standard 220V plug.
