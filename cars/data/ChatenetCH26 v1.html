Chatenet CH26 v1

The CH26 (first generation / v1) carries a quirky styling that many compare to a mashup of a Mini Cooper aesthetic with Italian styling cues. One article describes the 2009 CH26 as “a strange compound of Mini and a bit of Maserati.”

Dimensions: Length ~ 3,065 mm, width ~ 1,567 mm, height ~ 1,453 mm.

Because it’s a microcar / quadricycle class, weight is very low (some sources quote ~ 350 kg or so) to stay within regulatory limits.
