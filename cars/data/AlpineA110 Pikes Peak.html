Alpine A110 Pikes Peak

Alpine will participate for the first time in its history on 25 June 2023, in the Pikes Peak International Hill Climb, using a special variant of the A110 developed in collaboration with Signatech. It was unveiled in May 2023, featuring revised aerodynamics, a tuned engine producing nearly 500 hp (370 kW) and a weight of only 950 kg (2,090 lb).
