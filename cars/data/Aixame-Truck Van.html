Aixam e-Truck Van

The Aixam e-Truck Van has been in production since 2018 and continues to be manufactured as of 2025. This electric utility vehicle is part of Aixam's "Pro" lineup, designed specifically for urban logistics and tradespeople.

Launched in 2018, the e-Truck Van marked Aixam's commitment to sustainable, license-free commercial vehicles. With a 6.14 kWh lithium battery, it offers a range of up to 100 km per charge, making it ideal for short-distance urban deliveries. The vehicle features a compact design, providing 2.8 cubic meters of cargo space, and operates with zero noise pollution, aligning with eco-friendly urban transport initiatives.

In 2024, Aixam inaugurated a new production facility in Andancette, France, dedicated to assembling utility vehicles like the e-Truck Van. This move aims to enhance production capacity and efficiency, supporting the growing demand for electric commercial vehicles.

As of 2025, the e-Truck Van remains a vital component of Aixam's lineup, catering to businesses seeking compact, eco-friendly delivery solutions without the need for a driving license.
