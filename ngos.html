NGOs

These NGOs are recognized for their significant impact and contributions to various global issues.

BRAC

Founded in Bangladesh, BRAC is one of the world's largest NGOs, focusing on poverty alleviation through education, healthcare, and economic development.

Médecins Sans Frontières (Doctors Without Borders)

An international medical humanitarian organization providing emergency aid in conflict zones and areas affected by diseases.

Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation

A private foundation dedicated to enhancing healthcare, reducing extreme poverty, and expanding educational opportunities globally.

Oxfam International

A confederation of organizations working together to end poverty and injustice worldwide.

Save the Children

Focuses on improving the lives of children through better education, healthcare, and economic opportunities.

Wikimedia Foundation

The nonprofit organization behind Wikipedia, aiming to provide free and accessible knowledge to everyone.

Danish Refugee Council

Provides assistance to refugees and displaced persons, promoting durable solutions to displacement problems.

Ashoka

Supports social entrepreneurs with innovative solutions to social problems, fostering a global community of change-makers.

Mercy Corps

Works in disaster-stricken and economically weak areas to promote community-led development and resilience.

Cure Violence Global

Aims to reduce violence globally by treating it as a contagious disease and implementing health-based strategies.

No Kid Hungry

Focuses on ending child hunger in the United States by connecting children to effective nutrition programs.

Partners In Health

Provides healthcare to impoverished communities, aiming to bring the benefits of modern medical science to those most in need.
