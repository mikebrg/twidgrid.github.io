  • Deep in an arid desert, a reconnaissance drone crashes from the sky. The explosion rocks a secret underground military base, and a mysterious “hybrid” is awoken within. In another time or another world, the young Nadira and her pet lizard Jiji begin their journey through desert sands to find the mysterious oasis of Jala. According to the legend, this is where they will find Taishma, the Lost Princess, hidden in the Moon Palace - only she can fulfill Nadira’s deepest wish, and bring her little sister back to life. Mirage 7 is a dark fairytale, a third-person adventure with a unique blend of fantasy and science-fiction elements. Follow Nadira’s tale, as she journeys to reveal a secret that goes beyond her comprehension.
