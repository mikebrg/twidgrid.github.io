Destiny 2: Renegades

Destiny 2: Renegades is a medium-sized expansion for Destiny 2, a first-person shooter video game developed by Bungie. Representing the 10th expansion for the game, it was released December 2, 2025, for PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Windows, Xbox One, and Xbox Series X/S. It is the second expansion for Year 8 of the game, referred to as the "Year of Prophecy", following The Edge of Fate that released in July 2025.
