Showgunners

Do you like squishing gnats? Do you like games you can't lose? Do you want to fill your free time with the most inane activity possible? It doesn't matter what you answered, you should download Gnat Squisher! If you get bored with gnats, you can switch to other equally fun-to-squish objects. This app is the result of a Grinnell College Experimental College (ExCo) course conducted by Alex Exarhos ('10). Participants: Matt Doers, Isaiah Sarju, Xin Jin
