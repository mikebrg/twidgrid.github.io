  • You are Indiana Jones. Live the adventure as Indy in a thrilling story full of exploration, immersive action, and intriguing puzzles. You will travel the world in a race against enemy forces to discover the secrets to one of the greatest mysteries of all time. When a break-in in the dead of night ends in a confrontation with a mysterious colossal man, you must set out to discover the world-shattering secret behind the theft of a seemingly unimportant artifact. Travel from the halls of Marshall College to the heart of the Vatican, the pyramids of Egypt, the sunken temples of Sukhothai, and beyond. Use guile and wits to solve ancient riddles and survive intense set-pieces. Indiana’s trademark whip can be used for traversal as well as to distract, disarm, and attack enemies. Combine stealth infiltration, melee combat, and gunplay to combat the enemy threat and unravel the mystery. Venture through a mix of linear, narrative-driven gameplay and open-area maps. Indulge your inner explorer and unearth fascinating secrets, deadly traps and fiendish puzzles, where anything could potentially hide the next piece of the mystery.
