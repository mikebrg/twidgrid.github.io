Folklore Hunter

You are a hunter world renowned for slaying supernatural beasts. Your next contract is to hunt down a wendigo plaguing a section of woods in North America. Gather resources, set traps, prepare your equipment, use your hunters instincts and take down the beast, before it hunts you. Folklore hunter is intended to be shared with friends. The game has have full multiplayer Co-op capabilities with an unlimited number of friends. Gather your hunting party, work together and strategize and unlock the secrets of the forest.
