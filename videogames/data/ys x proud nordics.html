Ys X: Proud Nordics

Oland Island is a huge island located in the northern part of Obelia Bay. During a battle with immortal undead beings known as Griegers, red-haired adventurer Adol and pirate princess Carja visit the island and come across numerous ruins in an uncharted territory. What on earth happened on this island...? An encounter with another "Mana user" leads Adol and his friends on an unknown adventure. In addition to the exhilarating "Cross Action" between Adol and Carja, new "Mana Action" and the vast Oland Island map and exploration areas have been implemented! There are even more elements to keep you playing, such as new bosses, Mana Ride Races, and battles in the arena where deadly enemies await!
