Piece by Piece

It's time to open shop! Inherit your family's repair shop, and roll up your sleeves. Fix up the prized possessions of the forest folk, paint yourself a masterpiece to sell and make your family shop all your own with decorations, furniture and cookies! Customize your shop with different colours and materials, grow plants and mix paint, spruce up the items that pass through the store, bake cookies for your neighbours, and listen to the latest gossip - are you ready to roll up your sleeves and open up shop?
