Nicktoons & The Dice of Destiny

From the creative forces behind Nickelodeon All-Star Brawl 2, Nicktoons & The Dice of Destiny will take you on an epic fantasy quest with your favorite Nickelodeon characters. Journey across Nickelodeon-inspired fantasy lands on a grand quest inspired by tabletop RPGs in Nicktoons & The Dice of Destiny! Slash, bash, cast spells, and strategize your way to victory against a variety of enemies in fast-paced and fluid ARPG combat. Upgrade your weapons and master new abilities in solo play and local co-op for up to four players.
