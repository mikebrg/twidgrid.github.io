Reigns: The Witcher

Adventure as Geralt through the ballads of Dandelion in Reigns: The Witcher, the latest mutation of the smash-hit swipe ‘em up Reigns series from Nerial and Devolver Digital, set in the merciless, dark-fantasy world of CD PROJEKT RED’s award-winning The Witcher series. As Geralt of Rivia, legendary monster slayer, you will navigate the moral maze and fight for survival through the drunken ballads of the bard. Will you hunt monsters, upset the locals, or run a hot bath? Compose an inspiring epic to perhaps, one day, claim immortality.
