  • Erik the Red

    Known for founding the first Viking settlements in Greenland

    Born: c. 950 Jæren, Norway - Died: c. 1003 (aged around 53) Greenland

    He most likely earned the epithet "the Red" due to the color of his hair and beard.

    Allegiance: Norse settlers of Iceland and Greenland; Viking Age Scandinavian culture

    Conflicts: Feuds in Iceland leading to his exile; Regional disputes among Norse settlers in Greenland
