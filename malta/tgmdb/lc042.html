Qala Local Council

The Local Council of Qala is the local governing body of Qala, a small village located on the northeastern tip of the island of Gozo, Malta. Known for its scenic views, Qala is a picturesque area that combines a rich history with modern-day community life. The council is responsible for managing local services, promoting cultural heritage, and organizing events that celebrate the village's traditions and community spirit. With its close-knit population, the Local Council plays a central role in shaping the village's development, ensuring a balance between preserving its historic charm and supporting local growth.
