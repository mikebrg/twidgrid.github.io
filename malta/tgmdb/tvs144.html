Ħames Minuti

"Ħames Minuti" is a Maltese television show that focuses on discussing current events and societal issues in a quick and engaging format. The title, which translates to "Five Minutes," reflects the show's short duration, aiming to present important topics concisely. It often features interviews with experts, commentators, and public figures, giving viewers a brief but insightful look at trending issues in Malta. The program has gained popularity for its ability to deliver relevant content in a fast-paced and accessible way.
