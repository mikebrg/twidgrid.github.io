Għasri Local Council

The Local Council of Għasri is the governing body of the village of Għasri, located on the island of Gozo in Malta. Known as one of the smallest and most picturesque villages in the Maltese archipelago, Għasri is celebrated for its peaceful rural landscapes, traditional stone farmhouses, and charming community spirit. The council plays a vital role in preserving the village’s heritage, promoting sustainable development, and improving local infrastructure and public services. Għasri is particularly famous for the Wied il-Għasri valley, a narrow gorge popular for its scenic beauty and crystal-clear waters, drawing both locals and tourists seeking a tranquil escape into nature. Through its efforts, the Local Council of Għasri strives to maintain the village’s unique character while enhancing quality of life for residents and visitors alike.
