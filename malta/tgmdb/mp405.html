Camilleri, Benny

Benny Camilleri served as a Member of Parliament in Malta for both the Nationalist Party, during the First Legislature (1962-1966), and later the Labour Party, in the Fourth Legislature (1976-1981). There is also information about a current Minister, Benny Camilleri, who served as the Mayor of Fgura and later as the Minister for Home Affairs, National Security and Law Enforcement.
