Żebbuġ Rangers Football Club

Żebbuġ Rangers Football Club is a football club from the town of Żebbuġ in Malta. The club currently competes in the Maltese Challenge League. In the 2021-22 season they won the Maltese Challenge League after a win against Pembroke Athleta. Their current team manager is Jurgen D'Amato, who joined in 2021, and their assistant coach is Vincent Carbonaro.
