Regimental Sergeant Major

Regimental sergeant major (RSM) is an appointment that may be held by a warrant officer (WO) in the Maltese Army. Only one warrant officer holds the appointment of RSM in any regiment or battalion, making them the senior warrant officer; in a unit with more than one top-ranked WO, the RSM is considered to be first amongst equals'. The RSM is primarily responsible for assisting their commander in maintaining standards and discipline amongst the non-commissioned members and acts as a parental figure to their subordinates, sometimes referred to by the mantra 'Drill, Dress and Discipline'.
