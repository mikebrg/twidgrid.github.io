Ħal Lija Local Council

The Local Council of Ħal Lija, located in the central region of Malta, serves as the administrative body for the village of Lija. Known for its picturesque streets and charming architecture, Lija is home to several notable sites, including the stunning Church of St. Mary, which features a beautiful dome and intricate frescoes. The council is responsible for various local initiatives aimed at enhancing the community's quality of life, promoting cultural heritage, and preserving the environment. Lija is also celebrated for its lush gardens and vibrant community events, making it a beloved destination for both residents and visitors alike.
