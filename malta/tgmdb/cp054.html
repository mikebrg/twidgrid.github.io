Santa Venera, Saint Venera

The old church of Santa Venera was entrusted to the Carmelite monks in 1912 when there were not many people there. Six years later, Santa Venera became a parish. Because the population continued to increase, the monks felt the need to build another church in a more central location, in Triq San Guzepp. On 3 December 1989 the new church became the parish church and on 6 October 1990 the first stone of the new church was laid. It was blessed on July 17, 2005.
