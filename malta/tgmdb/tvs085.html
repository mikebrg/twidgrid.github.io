Feliċi

Feliċi is a popular Maltese TV show that combines comedy and drama to depict the ups and downs of everyday life. The series follows the lives of a group of characters living in Malta, with each episode focusing on relatable situations, from personal relationships to work struggles. It stands out for its lighthearted humor while also touching on deeper themes such as family, love, and friendship, making it a hit among viewers of all ages. With its authentic portrayal of Maltese culture, *Feliċi* has become a beloved part of the local TV scene.
