Gustavus S. Brander

It also became lawful for the Governor to order that the Department of Police be administered and governed by a board and to confer and assign to each or any or all members of the board any powers and duties which were by law or regulation conferred or assigned to the commissioner. Under this Ordinance, with effect from August 24, 1930, Colonel Gustav Brander, OBE, was appointed joint commissioner with Galea.
