Borg, Gontran

Gontran Borg was a former Labour Party Member of Parliament (MP) in Malta who served during the Fifth Legislature (1982-1987) and passed away in November 2015 at the age of 92. He was a member of the Malta Labour Party and was elected to Parliament on December 18, 1981.

Political Career: Borg was a Labour MP for the Fifth Legislature.

Electoral History: He was elected on December 18, 1981.

Death: Gontran Borg died on or around November 9, 2015.

Legacy: He was remembered by the Labour Party and the Association of Former Members of Parliament.
