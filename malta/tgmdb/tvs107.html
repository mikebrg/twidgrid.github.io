It-Tieġ ta' Karmena Abdilla

"It-Tieġ ta' Karmena Abdilla" is a popular Maltese television spectacle that aired on NET Television. The show is a comedy-drama, blending humor and heartwarming moments, revolving around the wedding of Karmena Abdilla, a beloved character in Maltese culture. The story highlights the chaos and excitement of wedding preparations, showcasing family dynamics, misunderstandings, and traditional Maltese customs. The production is well-known for its vibrant performances, catchy songs, and memorable characters, making it a staple in Maltese television entertainment.
