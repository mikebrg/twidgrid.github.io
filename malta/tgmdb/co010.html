ICPE

The Institute for World Evangelisation-ICPE Mission is a Catholic mission made up of Christians committed to the goal of world evangelisation. The ICPE Mission is dedicated to the formation and training of Catholics so that they may become more effective evangelisers. Through evangelisation, discipleship, community building and mercy work, the ICPE Mission pursues its purposes of training, formation, serving the church and affecting the world for Christ. The ICPE Mission is an international association of the faithful of pontifical right and the missionary expression of the Glory of God International Covenant Community.
