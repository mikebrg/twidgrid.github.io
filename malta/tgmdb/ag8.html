Edgar Mizzi

Edgar Mizzi was Attorney General of Malta from 1971 until 1981.

At the earlier stage (1965–1971) the post was called Crown Advocate General, and during that time, Edgar Mizzi held that title too (1965–1971) (as Crown Advocate General); then when the title changed to Attorney General (mid-1970s) he continued in the post.

His tenure covers a period in which Malta’s legal structure was evolving (changes in statutory titles etc.).
