Victoria Hotspurs Football Club

Victoria Hotspurs Football Club is a Maltese football club from the town of Victoria, Gozo, and that as from the 2024-25 season has become the first ever Gozitan club to switch from the Gozo football league system to the Maltese one. The club was founded in 1948 and, prior to their migration, has won 13 First Division titles, the last one being in the 2018-19 season.
