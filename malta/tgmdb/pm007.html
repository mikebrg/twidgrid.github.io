Giorgio Borġ Olivier

Giorgio Borg Olivier GCPO KSS (Maltese: Ġorġ Borg Olivier) (5 July 1911 - 29 October 1980) was a Maltese statesman and leading politician. He twice served as Prime Minister of Malta (1950-1955 and 1962-1971) as the Leader of the Nationalist Party. He was also Leader of the Opposition between 1955-1958, and again between 1971-1977.
