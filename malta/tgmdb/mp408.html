Holland, Patrick

Patrick Holland was a Maltese Member of Parliament for the Labour Party from 1962 until his resignation as a minister in 1983, serving in various roles including Parliamentary Secretary and Minister of Trade and Housing. He was also a minister from 1971-1983, holding positions such as Parliamentary Secretary under the Ministry of Justice and Housing, Minister of Housing and Lands, Minister of Trade, and Minister of Parastatal Investments. He passed away in 1986 at the age of 52.
