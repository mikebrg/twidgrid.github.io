Mir-Refittorju

Mir-Refittorju is a Maltese television show that airs on NET TV. The show explores themes of faith, personal struggles, and redemption, often featuring emotional and inspiring stories. With a strong focus on Christian values, *Mir-Refittorju* presents real-life experiences and thought-provoking discussions that resonate with its audience. The program aims to offer hope and encouragement, making it a significant part of NET TV's religious and social programming.
