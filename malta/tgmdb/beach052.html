Żonqor Point

Żonqor Point, also known as Ras iz-Zonqor, is located on the north side of the entrance to Marsaskala Bay in Malta. It’s a shore dive site with shallow reefs on both sides of the peninsula, and its maximum depth is around 20 meters. The reef on the south side of Żonqor Point is usually dived on the way back from the tugboat wrecks St. Michael and Tug 10.
