Luigi Federigo d'Aragona

Louis Fadrique (also Luis or Lluís Frederic d'Aragó; died 1382) a Catalan nobleman who was Count of Salona, as well as lord of various other towns in Central Greece from ca. 1365 until his death in 1382. In 1375-1381 he also served as the vicar-general of the twin duchy of Athens and Neopatras.
