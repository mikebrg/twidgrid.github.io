Telebejgħ

Telebejgħ is a Maltese television show aired on NET TV, focused on telemarketing and home shopping. The program showcases a variety of products, including household items, electronics, and personal care goods, with presenters providing detailed demonstrations and special offers. Designed to appeal to a broad audience, *Telebejgħ* allows viewers to purchase featured products conveniently via phone or online orders. It serves as a platform for local and international brands to reach Maltese consumers directly from their homes.
