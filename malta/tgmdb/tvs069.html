Il-Klinika

"Il-Klinika" is a Maltese television drama series that premiered on ONE TV in October 2023. The show was created and directed by Clayton Pace and consists of ten episodes that aired between October and December 2023. The narrative centers around a medical clinic, delving into the personal and professional lives of its staff and patients. The series explores various themes, including power dynamics, personal ambitions, and the ethical challenges faced by medical professionals.
