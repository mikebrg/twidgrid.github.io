Marsaxlokk Football Club

Marsaxlokk Football Club is a Maltese football club from the town of Marsaxlokk, which currently plays in the BOV Premier League. The club was founded in 1949. Marsaxlokk won the Maltese Premier League title for the first time in their history, after finishing as league champions for the 2006-07 season. Marsaxlokk has its headquarters at Triq il-Kavalleriza, Mxlokk redoubt.
