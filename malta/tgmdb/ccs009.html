St Albert the Great College

Our College caters for boys and girls from Kinder 1 up till senior school of ALL abilities, from different backgrounds, religions and gender types. We strive to ensure that all students are happy and that they realise their potential in all areas of the curriculum and beyond. We are proud to be part of the Valletta and Fgura community with their diverse cultural, historical and social aspects. St Albert the Great college is part of the Maltese Dominican Province.
