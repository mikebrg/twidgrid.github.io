Ara min Mar!

Ara Min Mar! is a popular Maltese television show that explores the fascinating world of genealogy and family history. Hosted by renowned presenter Angie Laus, the program takes viewers on an emotional journey as well-known personalities discover their ancestral roots. Through historical documents, DNA tests, and expert research, each episode uncovers surprising connections and long-lost family stories, making it a compelling and heartfelt watch for audiences in Malta and beyond.
