Victoria Local Council

The Local Council of Victoria, also known as Rabat, is the administrative authority for the capital city of Gozo, Malta's sister island. Situated in the heart of Gozo, the council oversees the governance and development of Victoria, which is known for its rich history, cultural heritage, and iconic landmarks such as the Citadel. The council focuses on community welfare, urban planning, and preserving the area’s historical charm while promoting tourism and sustainable development. Victoria serves as a hub for local events, festivals, and economic activities, making the council's role pivotal in balancing tradition with modern progress.
