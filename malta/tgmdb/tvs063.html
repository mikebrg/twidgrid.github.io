Għidli Storja

"Għidli Storja" (translated as "Tell Me a Story") is a Maltese animated series produced by Toontuloon, an animation studio based in Malta. Launched in 2012, it stands as Toontuloon's inaugural project and has garnered enduring affection from Maltese audiences. The series presents short animations that bring to life childhood memories recounted by seniors, offering a unique platform for storytelling.
