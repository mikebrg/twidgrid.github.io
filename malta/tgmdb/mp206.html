Refalo, Louis

Hon. Louis Refalo MP

Partit Nazzjonalista - Seventh Legislature (1992 - 1996)

Refalo served as a PN MP between 1981 and 1996 and had followed the footsteps of his father, Carmelo Refalo, who had also been a PN MP.

Refalo had also worked as a Director of the Sea Malta and of the Malta Freeport.
