Bremse Class

Bremse Class, P32, P33, Commissioned: 1992, Decommissioned: 2012/2005, Built at VEB Yachtwerft Berlin in 1971-1972, P33 was decommissioned in 2005 and was scuttled as an artificial reef in 2021. P32 was decommissioned in 2012, but it is still moored at Hay Wharf along with the other commissioned patrol boats as of 2015.
