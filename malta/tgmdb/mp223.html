Dingli, Frans

Frans Dingli was a Member of Parliament (MP) for the Nationalist Party (Partit Nazzjonalista) in Malta's Second Legislature, serving from 1966 to 1971. His service was part of a period when the Nationalist Party held significant political influence in the country.

Details of Frans Dingli's parliamentary service:

Political Party: Nationalist Party (Partit Nazzjonalista)

Legislative Period: Second Legislature (1966–1971)

Key Dates: Elected on March 31, 1966, and took the Oath of Allegiance on April 25, 1966.

Parliamentary Dissolution: Parliament was dissolved on April 24, 1971, at the end of his term.
