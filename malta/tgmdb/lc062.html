Xewkija Local Council

The Local Council of Xewkija, located on the island of Gozo in Malta, governs one of the island's oldest and most charming villages. Known for its historical and cultural significance, Xewkija is home to the iconic Rotunda of St. John the Baptist, which boasts one of the largest unsupported domes in Europe. The council plays a pivotal role in maintaining the village's infrastructure, promoting community well-being, and preserving its rich heritage. It actively supports local initiatives, events, and projects that celebrate Xewkija's traditions while fostering sustainable development and enhancing the quality of life for its residents.
