Iklin Local Council

The Local Council of Iklin is the governing body responsible for managing the small, charming town of Iklin in central Malta. Established in 1994, the council oversees community services, infrastructure maintenance, and environmental initiatives aimed at enhancing the quality of life for its residents. Iklin, known for its peaceful suburban atmosphere and historical significance, including archeological sites and old chapels, relies on the council to support its development while preserving its heritage. Through local events and initiatives, the council fosters a strong sense of community, encouraging resident participation in making Iklin a vibrant and welcoming place to live.
