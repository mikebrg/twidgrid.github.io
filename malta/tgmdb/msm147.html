Voir Malte et Mourir

Malta, 1979. While preparing to collect explosive documents from a certain Godfrey Borg, John Fitzpatrick, an MI5 agent 'subcontracted' by the CIA, mysteriously disappears. Shortly after, his body was recovered off the coast of Valletta. Everything indicates that the man was atrociously tortured. Eager to shed light on this unfortunate event, Langley immediately dispatched his best element, Herr Prinz Malko Linge, with the dual mission of identifying John Fitzpatrick's assassins and recovering the famous 'explosive' documents held by the mysterious Godfrey Borg. The problem is that it has evaporated into nature. Fortunately, His Highness has a lead to trace him! A track with a devastating neckline and an incendiary rump
