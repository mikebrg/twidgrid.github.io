Swift Class

Swift Class, P23 (ex-C23), P24 (ex-C24), Commissioned: 1971, Decommissioned: 2010, Built in 1967 as USA C6823 (PCF-813) and USA C6824 (PCF-816). Transferred from the United States Coast Guard in 1971. In 2012 P24 was handed back to the USA and it is now in the Maritime Museum of San Diego. P23 is laid up at Hay Wharf as of 2014.
