Zebbug Tennis Club

Situated strategically within an established school, that is, St Dorothy’s School, the club comprises of four tennis courts each having their own floodlights. Besides private individual coaching there is also Sunday and weekday coaching both for children and adults starting of with beginners up to advanced level. A fast method of teaching called mini-tennis whereby specialised balls and racquets will also be offered for the younger members. Also over five hundred members are already affiliated to the club. Most importantly to note is that only qualified coaches will coach at this club.
