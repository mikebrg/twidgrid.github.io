Fuq Fomm Kulħadd

"Fuq Fomm Kulħadd" is a Maltese television series that delves into historical events and figures that have significantly impacted Malta, bringing to light stories that have been widely discussed among the Maltese populace. The title translates to "On Everyone's Lips," reflecting the show's focus on topics that have been prominent in public discourse. Each episode of "Fuq Fomm Kulħadd" explores a different subject, providing viewers with in-depth narratives that combine historical research with engaging storytelling. For instance, one episode examines the story of Carmelo Borg Pisani, a young Maltese man who lived in Italy and, during the height of World War II in 1942, volunteered for a espionage mission in Malta. His actions and subsequent fate became a topic of widespread discussion in the years that followed.
