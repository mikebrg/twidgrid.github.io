Castaldi Paris, Ian

Ian Castaldi Paris is a Maltese politician from the Labour Party who served as a member of the Parliament of Malta. Castaldi Paris was elected in a casual election in District 8 in May 2020. In December 2020 he was fined over 300,000 euros for tax issues. As a result, he was removed from the public accounts committee. He was replaced by Jonathan Attard. He stood down at the 2022 Maltese general election.
