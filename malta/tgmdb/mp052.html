Bezzina, Toni

Toni Bezzina is a Maltese politician from the Nationalist Party. He was elected to the Parliament of Malta in the 2022 Maltese general election as an additional member to compensate for party disproportionality. In 2021, Bezzina was cleared by a police investigation into alleged corruption dating back to 2012. In 2017, he was a candidate for deputy leader of the Nationalist Party.
