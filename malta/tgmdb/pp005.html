Alliance of Liberal Democrats Malta

The Alliance of Liberal Democrats Malta (Alleanza Liberali-Demokratika Malta) or ALDM is a political pressure group in Malta. It was founded by Dr. John Zammit in 2006 as a split from the Alpha Partit Liberali (Alpha Liberal Party) he had co-founded with Dr. Emmy Bezzina in 2004. The ALDM advocates for issues such as divorce, abortion, gay marriage, and women’s rights.
