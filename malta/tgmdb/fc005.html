Birżebbuġa Windmill Football Club

Home to the beautiful seaside village of Birzebbuga (Malta), Birzebbuga Windmill F.C.N was established in 1972 and is one of the founding football nurseries on the island. The club is affiliated with Malta Youth Football Association - a branch of Malta Football Association. The nursery’s parent club is Birzebbuga St. Peter’s F.C. Besides regular training sessions, the club participates in various football events, festivals, tournaments and leagues throughout the year. It also provides frequent opportunities to participate in competitive tournaments abroad.
