Democratic Alternative

Democratic Alternative (Maltese: Alternattiva Demokratika), sometimes referred to as AD - The Green Party, was a green political party in Malta. The party was initially founded by a coalition of former Labour Party members and environmental activists in 1989. On 1 August 2020 the party announced a plan to merge with the Democratic Party to form a new party called AD+PD. The merger was conducted on 17 October 2020.
