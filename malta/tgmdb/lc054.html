Siġġiewi Local Council

The Local Council of Siġġiewi is the local governing body for the town of Siġġiewi, located in the central part of Malta. This council is responsible for overseeing various aspects of local governance, including urban planning, public services, and community initiatives. Siġġiewi, known for its rich history and picturesque rural surroundings, has a strong local identity and heritage, which the council works to preserve and promote. The council also plays a key role in organizing events and fostering a sense of community among residents. With its mix of tradition and modernity, the Local Council of Siġġiewi continues to serve as an essential part of the town’s development and well-being.
