Buontempo, Stefan

Parliamentary Secretary for Research, Innovation, Youth and Sport

Ministry for Education and Employment

Born: 16 May 1962 to Mary and John in Valletta and has one brother and two sisters. His father is the late Prof. John Buontempo who served as ambassador to various Arab and Middle Eastern countires, and single-handed worked tirelessly to solve the Lockerbie trial impasse. He is married to Natasha, a lawyer and has two children Giovanni and Beppe

Education: He studied architecture in Cardiff and California

Work experience: 1996 2003 He was appointed Consultant to the Minister of Housing and Local Councils

He became a member of parliament and championed the social justice cause particularly social housing. In the previous legislature he also served as Shadow Minister for Local Councils.
