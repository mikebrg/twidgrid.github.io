Cauchi, Amabile

Amabile Cauchi was a Maltese Member of Parliament who represented the Partit Nazzjonalista (Nationalist Party) in the Parliament of Malta during the Second, Third, and Fourth Legislatures (1966-1981). He passed away on October 10, 1995.

Key Details

Name: Amabile Cauchi
Political Party: Partit Nazzjonalista (Nationalist Party)
Legislative Terms: Second Legislature: 1966-1971, Third Legislature: 1971-1976, Fourth Legislature: 1976-1981
Date of Death: October 10, 1995
