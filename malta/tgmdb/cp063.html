Ta’ Xbiex, Saint John of the Cross

The Teresian Carmelite Friars started building their convent in Ta' Xbiex in 1948 on a piece of land that was given to them by Baron Pietro Paolo Testaferrata Moroni Viani. The church began to be used in 1958 and became a Parish on the first of June 1969. It was consecrated on the 1st of June 1989.
