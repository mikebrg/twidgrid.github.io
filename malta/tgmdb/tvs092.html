Fuq 3 Faċċati

"Fuq 3 Faċċati" is a popular Maltese television show that airs on TVM. The show offers a mix of comedy and social commentary, exploring a wide range of topics that resonate with local audiences. With its unique format, it features a blend of sketches, interviews, and discussions, often poking fun at the everyday realities and quirks of Maltese society. The show is well-loved for its witty humor and engaging approach to addressing contemporary issues in a light-hearted yet thought-provoking manner.
