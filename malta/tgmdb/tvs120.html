Nitfa Vuċi għall-Annimali

Nitfa Vuċi għall-Annimali is a Maltese television show aired on NET TV that focuses on animal welfare and rights. The program raises awareness about various issues affecting animals in Malta, including rescue stories, adoption opportunities, and responsible pet ownership. Through interviews with experts and real-life cases, the show educates viewers on how to better care for animals and advocate for their well-being. It serves as an important platform for promoting kindness and compassion towards all creatures.
