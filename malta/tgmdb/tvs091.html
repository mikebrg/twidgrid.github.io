Fuq 3

"Fuq 3" is a Maltese television program that airs every Saturday at 3:00 PM on NET Television. Hosted by Charlene Buttigieg Farrugia, Chris X. Grech, and Maria Francalanza, the show offers a variety of segments, including interviews with experts, surprise visits to personalities, and satirical sketches featuring the character Tur Kritt. The program aims to entertain and inform viewers with its diverse content.
