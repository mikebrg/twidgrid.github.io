Żejtun Local Council

The Local Council of Żejtun, located in the southeastern region of Malta, serves as the administrative body for this historic town. Żejtun, known for its rich cultural heritage and traditional Maltese architecture, is often referred to as "Città Beland." The council oversees various community services, including maintenance of public spaces, organization of cultural events, and promotion of local heritage. Żejtun is celebrated for its olive oil production history, charming parish church, and vibrant annual festivals like the Żejt iż-Żejtun, which highlights the town's agricultural legacy. The council works to preserve these traditions while fostering community development and modern amenities.
