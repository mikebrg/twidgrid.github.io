Int tagħmilha?

"Int tagħmilha?" is a popular Maltese television interview show aired on NET TV. The show features in-depth conversations with prominent personalities from various fields, including politics, entertainment, and business. Hosted by skilled journalists, the program provides viewers with insightful discussions and personal stories, often addressing current events and social issues in Malta. The format is known for its relaxed and engaging atmosphere, allowing guests to share their views and experiences in a candid manner.
