Tennis Club Kordin

Tennis Club Kordin is a public tennis facility located in Paola, in the south of Malta. We have 4 hard courts, fully equipped with LED lights, right next to a clubhouse for spectators. TCK is home to multiple local tournaments, with the TCK Open, the Juniors Open and the ITF international tournaments being the highlight of the year. Moreover, the club is equipped with an AED, allows online booking for members and even fingerprint access to make use of all time slots.
