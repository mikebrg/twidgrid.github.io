Radio 105

Establishing its presence in the broadcasting landscape, Radio 105, also known as Radio Galaxy, embarked on its journey on December 6, 2001. This milestone was achieved following the issuance of a license by the Broadcasting Authority to Mr. Manuel Chircop. The license specifically authorized community-based broadcasting, designating frequency 105 for Radio 105’s transmissions. Notably, the Malta Communications Authority facilitated this by releasing the same frequency to Mr. Chircop, laying the foundation for a community-centric radio station.
