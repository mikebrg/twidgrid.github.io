St. Paul's Bay Local Council

The Local Council of St Paul's Bay is a local government body responsible for the administration and development of the town of St Paul's Bay, located on the northern coast of Malta. Known for its beautiful coastline, vibrant community, and historical landmarks, the council works to enhance the quality of life for its residents and improve public services. It oversees various local initiatives, including infrastructure projects, cultural events, and environmental conservation efforts, aiming to balance urban growth with sustainability. St Paul's Bay is also a popular tourist destination, attracting visitors with its picturesque views and rich history, including the famous St. Paul's Bay where the apostle is believed to have been shipwrecked.
