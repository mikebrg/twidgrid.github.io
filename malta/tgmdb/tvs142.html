What's Cooking with Manuel

What's Cooking with Manuel is a popular Maltese television show hosted by Manuel. The show features a blend of cooking, entertainment, and local culture, as Manuel showcases various recipes and cooking techniques while engaging with guests and offering insights into Maltese cuisine. It is known for its lighthearted and fun atmosphere, making it a favorite among viewers who enjoy cooking, food, and a good dose of humor. The program highlights local ingredients and traditions, making it both educational and entertaining for its audience.
