Owen Luellen

Owen Leuellen is a Maltese Rapper and Songwriter. He rose to fame across the Maltese islands after placing 2nd on the X Factor Malta in 2019. Leuellen started writing songs at the age of 15 and after five years he made his first appearance on television on the X Factor. In the finals of the XFactor Owen was featured on a track with one of the show’s judges Ira Losco. He wrote his part on the track ‘HEY NOW’ which went VIRAL in days on all local radio stations and media platforms. The song reached a local milestone with 1.5 Million views on YouTube, which is three times the population of Malta. This experience paved the way for Owen to kick-start his career.
