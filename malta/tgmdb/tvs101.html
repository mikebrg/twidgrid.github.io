Il-Vjaġġ sat-Tieġ

Il-Vjaġġ sat-Tieġ is a popular Maltese television show that follows the journey of a couple preparing for their wedding. It highlights the challenges and emotional moments leading up to their big day, showcasing the cultural and personal aspects of the wedding process in Malta. The show combines elements of drama, humor, and romance, drawing in viewers with its relatable and heartfelt storyline. It has become a favorite among audiences for its realistic portrayal of the joys and struggles couples face during this significant life event.
