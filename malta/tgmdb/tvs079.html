Dawra sal-Festa

"Dawra Sal-Festa" is a popular Maltese TV show that revolves around the local traditions and cultural celebrations in Malta, particularly its vibrant village festas. The show captures the essence of these events, showcasing the preparations, rituals, and the strong sense of community that comes with them. It explores the significance of these festivals, which are an important part of Maltese heritage, blending religious, social, and artistic elements into one festive atmosphere. Through engaging storytelling, "Dawra Sal-Festa" highlights the joy, unity, and cultural pride that these celebrations bring to the islands.
