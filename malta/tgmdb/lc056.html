Swieqi Local Council

The Local Council of Swieqi is a local government body in the town of Swieqi, located on the island of Malta. It is responsible for managing and overseeing various community services, including public spaces, environmental initiatives, and local events. Swieqi is a residential area that has seen rapid development over the years, with a growing population and an expanding infrastructure. The Local Council plays a key role in enhancing the quality of life for its residents by ensuring the proper upkeep of the area, promoting social and cultural activities, and fostering a sense of community within this vibrant town.
