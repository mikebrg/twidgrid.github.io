Sannat Local Council

The Local Council of Sannat is a local government authority located in the southern part of Gozo, Malta's sister island. Sannat is a charming village known for its picturesque countryside, traditional limestone architecture, and panoramic views of the Mediterranean Sea. The local council is responsible for managing public services, community events, and maintaining the village's cultural heritage. It plays an important role in promoting local development, preserving the environment, and ensuring the well-being of the village’s residents, while also fostering tourism and cultural activities that showcase the rich history and traditions of Sannat.
