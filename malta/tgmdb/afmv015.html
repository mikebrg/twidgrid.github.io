Kondor I Class

Kondor I Class, P29, P30, P31, Commissioned: 1997/1992, Decommissioned: 2004, Built at Peene Werft Shipyard 1968-1970 as minesweepers with the following names: Boltenhagen (P29), Ueckermuende (P30), Pasewalk (P31), P29 and P31 were sunk as diving sites in 2007 and 2009. As of 2013, ex-P30 was laid up at Marsa.
