Għeruq

"Għeruq" is a Maltese television drama series that delves into the intricate lives of a high-society family and their servants in the period leading up to World War II. The narrative centers on a forbidden romance between a servant and the Baron's son, exploring themes of love, societal norms, and the profound impact of past events on present-day relationships. The series skillfully intertwines historical context with contemporary issues, such as inheritance disputes, corruption, and the tension between tradition and progress.
