Gudja Local Council

The Local Council of Gudja, located in the southeastern region of Malta, serves the historic town known for its unique triple-domed parish church dedicated to the Assumption of Mary. This council oversees the local administration, public services, and community initiatives aimed at preserving Gudja’s rich cultural heritage and supporting residents’ needs. Gudja, with its quiet, charming streets and centuries-old buildings, is surrounded by agricultural land and notable sites, including the impressive Casa d’Auriel and the remains of a Roman villa. The council plays a vital role in enhancing local infrastructure, organizing cultural events, and ensuring that Gudja retains its traditional character while embracing modern improvements for the benefit of its community.
