Gwardamanġa, Our Lady of Fatima

In 1952 the Dominican friars, with the encouragement of their provincial at the time P. Esmond Klimeck, built the Sanctuary of Our Lady of Fatima in Gwardamanġa, to be the Center of the Rosary Crusade . The Sanctuary was opened in 1955 and in 1968 it became a parish church.
