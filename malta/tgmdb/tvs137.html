Ulied ix-Xefaq

Ulied ix-Xelaq (literally translated to Children of the Horizon), is a new local series featuring an already-beloved band of adventurers and naturalists. Malta’s very own Exterminator Arnold Sciberras, along with scientific author and botanist Jeffrey Sciberras, will be joining popular presenter and adrenaline junkie Stephanie Chircop on a journey to the Aegadian Islands, a mostly overlooked archipelago off the northwestern coast of Sicily.
