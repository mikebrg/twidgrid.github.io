Imkejjen Storiċi

"Imkejjen Storiċi" is a Maltese television show that takes viewers on a journey through the island's rich history and culture. The program explores historical sites, landmarks, and monuments, offering an in-depth look at Malta's past and the stories behind its most iconic locations. Through engaging storytelling and expert insights, "Imkejjen Storiċi" highlights the significance of these places in shaping the nation's heritage, making it a captivating experience for history enthusiasts and curious viewers alike.
