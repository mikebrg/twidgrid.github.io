Ħaż-Żebbuġ Local Council

The Local Council of Ħaż-Żebbuġ, a historic town in central Malta, plays a vital role in managing the town's civic and administrative needs. Known for its rich cultural heritage and vibrant community, the council oversees various services, including waste management, road maintenance, and the organization of local events. Ħaż-Żebbuġ is renowned for its baroque architecture, picturesque streets, and the annual festa dedicated to St. Philip of Agira. The council also promotes local traditions and initiatives aimed at enhancing the quality of life for residents while preserving the town's historical significance.
