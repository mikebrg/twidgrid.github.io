Camilleri, Giuseppe Maria

Giuseppe Maria Camilleri (17 September 1915 – 29 July 1989) was a Maltese politician with the Nationalist Party who served as speaker of the House of Representatives between December 1953 and December 1954.

He was the son of judge Luigi Camilleri.

He first became a member of the Maltese parliament in 1948, when he stood for that year's general election, with the Nationalist Party.

When parliament was reconstituted in 1962, he again stood for election with the Nationalist Party and was elected as a member of the chamber on the government side. After this, he also successfully stood in the general elections of 1966, 1971, and 1976.
