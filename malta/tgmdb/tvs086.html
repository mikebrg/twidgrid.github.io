Fil-Moħba tal-Appostli

Fil-Moħba tal-Appostli is a Maltese TV show that explores the early history of Christianity in Malta, focusing on the lives of the apostles and the spread of Christian teachings on the island. The show blends historical fiction with spiritual themes, offering viewers a dramatic retelling of biblical events. With a mix of local and international talent, it highlights Malta's rich religious heritage and its role in shaping Christianity in the Mediterranean. Through engaging storytelling, the show brings to life the challenges and triumphs of the apostles as they journey through faith.
