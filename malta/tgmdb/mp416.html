Caruana, Carmelo

Carmelo Caruana was a Member of Parliament in Malta, representing the Partit Nazzjonalista (Nationalist Party) from 1962 to 1981. He served as Minister of Agriculture, Power, and Communications during the First Legislature (1962-1966) and held ministerial roles in earlier years as well.

Key Details

Name: Carmelo Caruana
Party: Partit Nazzjonalista (Nationalist Party)
Legislatures Served: First (1962–1966), Second (1966–1971), Third (1971–1976), and Fourth (1976–1981)
Notable Roles: Minister of Agriculture, Power, and Communications (First Legislature), Minister of Industry and Commerce (1950s), Minister of Agriculture, Energy, and Works (1962)
