Aladin

In Malta, the classic tale of Aladdin has been brought to life through various theatrical productions, captivating audiences with their unique interpretations. In December 2023, Teatru Astra in Gozo presented "Aladdin: The Rock N' Roll Panto," produced by FM Theatre Productions. This vibrant performance combined the traditional story with the energy of a rock and roll concert, featuring a talented ensemble of actor-musicians.
