Xgħajra Local Council

The Local Council of Xgħajra, a charming seaside village in Malta, serves as the administrative body for this small but vibrant community. Nestled along the island's eastern coast, Xgħajra is known for its picturesque views of the Mediterranean Sea and its tranquil environment. The council is responsible for maintaining local infrastructure, fostering community development, and organizing cultural events that celebrate the area's unique heritage. Despite its modest size, Xgħajra is a close-knit community that balances modern living with traditional Maltese charm, offering residents and visitors alike a peaceful and scenic retreat.
