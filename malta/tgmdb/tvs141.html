Vuċi għall-Annimali - Adozzjoni

Vuċi għall-Annimali - Adozzjoni is a Maltese television show that focuses on promoting animal adoption. It highlights the importance of adopting pets in need of loving homes, showcasing animals from various shelters in Malta. The program aims to raise awareness about animal welfare, featuring stories of rescued animals and their journeys to finding new families. Through heartfelt stories and compelling visuals, *Vuċi għall-Annimali - Adozzjoni* encourages viewers to consider adopting instead of buying, helping to give abandoned animals a second chance at life.
