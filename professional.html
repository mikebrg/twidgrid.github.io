Professional & Career

Job boards, professional networking sites, freelancing platforms, portfolio sites, and company websites.

LinkedIn

A professional networking platform for career development, job searching, and industry connections, LinkedIn also offers learning resources and company insights.

Indeed

One of the largest job search engines, aggregating job listings from multiple sites and company pages, with tools for job applications, resume building, and salary comparisons.

Glassdoor

Known for company reviews by employees, salary information, and job listings, Glassdoor helps job seekers gain insights into company culture and benefits.

Monster

A global job search platform that connects employers and job seekers, offering resources for resume building, career advice, and job application tracking.

ZipRecruiter

A user-friendly job board that provides job recommendations, allows easy applications, and connects job seekers with recruiters and hiring managers.

CareerBuilder

A long-standing platform offering job listings, resume building, and career advice, as well as career assessment tools and resources for professional growth.

SimplyHired

An extensive job search engine that lists opportunities from company websites and job boards, with resources for salary estimates and career planning.

Upwork

A freelancing platform that connects clients with freelancers in various fields, including design, writing, programming, and marketing, enabling contract-based work.

AngelList

A site focused on startup jobs, it connects job seekers with positions at emerging tech companies and offers options for remote and equity-based roles.

Hired

A platform that helps tech talent find job opportunities with top companies, offering a streamlined process where companies reach out directly to candidates.

FlexJobs

Specializing in remote and flexible jobs, FlexJobs provides verified listings in diverse career areas, ideal for those seeking work-life balance.

The Muse

A career resource that offers job listings, company insights, and personalized career advice, plus guidance on career paths, work environments, and professional development.
