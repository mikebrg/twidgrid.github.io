Jamaica

The Jamaica national football team, nicknamed the "Reggae Boyz", represents Jamaica in men's international football. The squad is under the supervising body of the Jamaica Football Federation (JFF), which is a member of the Caribbean Football Union (CFU), Confederation of North, Central American and Caribbean Association Football (CONCACAF), and the global jurisdiction of FIFA.
