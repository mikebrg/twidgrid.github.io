Tunisia

The Tunisia national football team, controlled by the Tunisian Football Federation (TFF), represents Tunisia in men's international Association football competitions. On a continental level, the team competes under the Confederation of African Football (CAF). It is also affiliated with FIFA for global competitions. Additionally, the team is a member of the Union of North African Football (UNAF) and the Union of Arab Football Associations (UAFA).
