Health

These platforms offer a mix of medical, holistic, and lifestyle-oriented advice, making them valuable resources for varied health and wellness needs.

Mayo Clinic

A trusted resource for medical and health information, offering tools like symptom trackers, disease insights, and the latest research updates. It's well-regarded for its evidence-based and accessible approach.

WebMD

Known for its detailed symptom-checking tools, medication interaction checkers, and health advice on various conditions. It’s a go-to for quick health-related queries.

Healthline

Offers comprehensive health articles, tips for managing medical conditions, and general wellness content tailored to various age groups and demographics.

Cleveland Clinic

A leading healthcare provider's website with medical news, health articles, and practical advice from top specialists.

Verywell Health

Focused on providing clear, evidence-backed content to improve understanding and management of health issues.

National Institutes of Health (NIH)

The U.S. government's health resource for the latest updates, medical news, and research-backed wellness advice.

Dr. Andrew Weil

Specializes in alternative medicine and holistic health, providing natural remedies, dietary tips, and wellness practices.

Everyday Health

Focuses on practical health tips, fitness guidance, and up-to-date news on medical conditions and treatments.

American Heart Association

Dedicated to heart health, with resources on diet, exercise, and managing cardiovascular diseases.

MindBodyGreen

Offers a blend of articles on fitness, nutrition, mental health, and sustainable living, with a focus on holistic wellness.

Well+Good

Focuses on lifestyle, self-care, and fitness trends, blending health advice with actionable wellness practices.

Livestrong

Provides fitness routines, meal plans, and motivation to support an active and healthy lifestyle.
