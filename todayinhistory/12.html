December

The month with the shortest day of the year in the Northern Hemisphere! This occurs during the winter solstice, which typically happens around December 21st or 22nd. On this day, the Earth's tilt causes the North Pole to be farthest from the Sun, leading to the least amount of daylight and the longest night of the year.
