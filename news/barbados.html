Barbados News

Stay Informed, Bajan Style – Your Guide to Barbados's Top News Sources.

Home Search

  • Bajan Reporter

    A national/regional news site recognized for its focus on current events, arts, and investigative pieces.

  • Barbados Today

    A privately-owned multimedia outlet known for its clean site layout and wide-ranging coverage: politics, business, entertainment, and special features.

  • Barbados Underground

    An alternative news and commentary site that often tackles stories underrepresented in mainstream media.

  • Caribbean Broadcasting Corporation (CBC)

    Barbados’s government-run radio & TV service, delivering national news via broadcast and online formats.

  • CaribVision

    An internationally broadcast channel with Caribbean-wide news anchored in Barbados, available via CBC.

  • Loop Barbados

    Part of the regional “Loop News” network, offering local coverage on lifestyle, business, entertainment, and happenings.

  • Nation News

    Barbados’s leading daily newspaper, offering strong coverage across politics, business, sports, lifestyle, and more.

  • The Barbados Advocate

    The country’s second most-read daily (in publication since 1895), covering local affairs, regional developments, and opinion pieces.
