  • Midtown Manhattan Nightclub Shooting: one killed, shooter at large

    2025, November 25

    United States

    On Sunday, outside of Harbor NYC Club in Hell's Kitchen, New York there was a shooting. No arrests have been made. The victim who was shot was taken to the hospital. He was shot in the back and groin. He died at the hospital. The shooting happened near the Intrepid Museum.

    A performance by the rapper Gunna was getting over when the man was shot. A potential witness was interviewed outside a 24-hour car wash. The nightclub is in the district of Manhattan, close to the Hudson River side (12th Ave).
