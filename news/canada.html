Canada News

Stay Informed with Canada's Top News Sources — All in One Place.

Search

  • CBC

    As the digital arm of the Canadian Broadcasting Corporation, CBC.ca offers comprehensive coverage of national and international news, politics, arts, and more. It's one of Canada's most visited news websites, boasting over 9 million monthly search visits.

  • CTV News

    Owned by Bell Media, CTV News provides extensive coverage on national and international events, politics, health, and entertainment. It's among the top news websites in Canada, with approximately 7.1 million monthly search visits.

  • Global News

    Global News delivers breaking news and in-depth analysis on Canadian and international stories. It's a significant player in the Canadian news landscape, attracting around 2.7 million monthly search visits.

  • Journal de Montréal

    A popular tabloid-style newspaper in Quebec, it covers local and national news, sports, and entertainment, with nearly 9 million monthly visits.

  • La Presse

    La Presse is a major French-language digital newspaper based in Montreal, offering in-depth coverage on various topics. It attracts over 10 million monthly visits.

  • National Post

    Offering a conservative-leaning perspective, the National Post covers national news, politics, and business. It's a prominent national newspaper in Canada.

  • Radio-Canada

    The French-language counterpart to CBC, Radio-Canada.ca provides news and information for Francophone Canadians. It's among the most visited news websites in Canada, with over 14 million monthly visits.

  • The Globe and Mail

    Known for its authoritative journalism, The Globe and Mail covers business, politics, and national affairs. It's one of Canada's most respected newspapers, with a weekly readership exceeding 6 million.

  • The Tyee

    An independent online news magazine based in Vancouver, The Tyee focuses on politics, culture, and life, offering in-depth analysis and investigative reporting.

  • Toronto Star

    The Toronto Star is renowned for its investigative journalism and progressive editorial stance. It's one of Canada's leading newspapers, with a significant online presence.
