Pentateuch

The Pentateuch is the first five books of the Bible, which tell the story of creation, the early history of humanity, and God's laws given to the Israelites through Moses.

Historical Books

The Bible's historical books tell the story of Israel's history, from entering the Promised Land to their exile and return, showing how God guided and judged His people over time.

Prophetic Books

The prophetic books of the Bible are writings by prophets who conveyed God's messages, often about future events, calling people to repentance, justice, and faithfulness.

Gospels

The Gospels in the Bible (Matthew, Mark, Luke, and John) are four books that tell the story of the life, teachings, death, and resurrection of Jesus Christ.
