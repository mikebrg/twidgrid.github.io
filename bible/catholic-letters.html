Catholic Letters

The Catholic Letters in the Bible are a collection of epistles written by various apostles, meant to offer guidance, encouragement, and teachings to early Christian communities.

Home Search Pentateuch Historical Books Wisdom Books Prophetic Books Gospels New Testament Letters Catholic Letters

  • James

    Teaches that genuine faith is shown through good works.

  • 1 Peter

    Offers hope and guidance to Christians facing persecution.

  • 2 Peter

    Warns against false teachers and reminds believers of Christ’s return.

  • 1 John

    Stresses love and fellowship with God and others as marks of true faith.

  • 2 John

    Encourages truth and love while warning against false teachings.

  • 3 John

    Praises hospitality and faithfulness within the Christian community.

  • Jude

    Warns against false teachers and urges believers to stay faithful.

  • Revelation

    A vision of the end times, promising victory for Christ and a new creation for believers.
