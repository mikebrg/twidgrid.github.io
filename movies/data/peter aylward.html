  • Peter Aylward

  • Years Active: 1977-present

  • Peter Aylward is an actor, best known for Spider-Man, Dream Within a Dream, Personal Foul, and The Untouchables. Other film credits include My Blind Brother, Lonely Hearts, and Meat Loaf: To Hell and Back.

    Theatre credits include Off-Broadway, as King Lear/Curan in King Lear (Union Square Theatre); and regionally, as Richardetto in 'Tis Pity She's a Whore (Goodman Theatre), King Philip in King Lear (Chicago Shakespeare Theater), Ghost of Hamlet’s Father in Hamlet (Ohio Theatre), and Count Almaviva in The Marriage of Figaro (Northlight Theatre).
