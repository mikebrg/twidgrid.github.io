  • Rachel Pizzolato (born January 19, 2004), is an American inventor, influencer, actress and model.

    In 2017, Pizzolato, while an eighth-grader attending John Curtis Christian School, was the winner of the Grand Sweepstakes Award in the Junior Division of the Greater New Orleans Science and Engineering Fair. She is known for appearing on the television series MythBusters Jr. in 2019.

    In 2025 she co-starred in the horror film Round the Decay.

    In the same year Pizzolato was the subject of a viral video where she tasted paan, an Indian after-meal practice. In the video Pizzolato stated that the dish was too much for her to handle.

    In July 2025 she was featured in an article in Maxim magazine where she spoke of not being a typical model and her desire to collaborate with brands such as AutoZone or Home Depot to pursue her passions for home repairs and working on cars, as well a desire to work with luxury brands such as Louis Vuitton and Chanel.

    Pizzolato is a Catholic and stands at 6 foot 1 inch (1.85 m) tall. In 2017 she was declared a national champion for her trampoline routine at a competition in Milwaukee, Wisconsin.

    Pizzolato also has patents on her two inventions, one involving vertical axis wind turbine energy and the other in relation to National Football League helmet technology.

    Since 2025, she is dating YouTuber Julian Georges Elchakieh, also known as Jmancurly.
