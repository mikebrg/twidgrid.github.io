  • Miku Patricia Martineau (born September 13, 2004) is a Canadian actress, voiceover artist, and singer. She gained prominence for her role as Ani in the film Kate (2021) and has since appeared in various film and television projects, including Honor Society (2022), Star Trek: Section 31 (2025), and the Netflix series Bet (2025).

    Martineau was born in Toronto, Ontario, Canada, to Peter Martineau, a voice director, and Kumiko Martineau, who is of Japanese descent. She pursued formal training at the Randolph College for the Performing Arts in Toronto, where she studied acting, singing, and dancing.

    In 2022, Martineau appeared as Christine in the teen comedy Honor Society. By 2025, she transitioned to science fiction, playing a young Philippa Georgiou in Star Trek: Section 31, a Paramount+ film that premiered January 24, 2025, exploring the backstory of Michelle Yeoh’s character.
