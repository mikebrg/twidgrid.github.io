Unchosen (2026)

A psychological thriller set in a conservative Christian cult, "Unchosen" follows the story of Rosie (Windsor), a dutiful wife and mother who is set on a dangerous path of emancipation and sexual awakening when she has an encounter with Sam (Fee), an escaped convict.
