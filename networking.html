Professional Networking Sites

These platforms offer diverse opportunities for professional networking, catering to various industries and networking preferences.

LinkedIn

The largest professional networking platform globally, enabling users to connect with colleagues, search for job opportunities, and share industry insights. 

Xing

A professional networking site popular in German-speaking countries, focusing on business networking and job opportunities.

Meetup

A platform that brings people together in person to learn and share experiences around common interests, including professional development.

Jobcase

A social platform dedicated to the workforce, empowering individuals to navigate the future of work.

Opportunity Network

A business network that uses an algorithm to match professionals with sales, job, and networking opportunities.

Bark

A platform connecting professionals with potential clients seeking their services.

Shapr

A networking app that connects professionals with similar interests for meaningful conversations.

Wellfound

A platform for startups to meet investors, talent, and incubators.

Slack Communities

Online communities within Slack where professionals can network, share knowledge, and collaborate.

Reddit

While primarily a discussion platform, subreddits like r/Networking and r/Career offer professional networking opportunities.

Facebook Groups

Various groups focused on professional development, industry-specific discussions, and job opportunities.

LunchMeet

An app that connects professionals for networking over lunch or coffee.
